Project name: Coi Cafe store
 Coi Cafe is a pire Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, famous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model (using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly form Dak Lak farmers). Coi's management decided to implement and expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.
- Scope:
+ Surveying and selecting premises
+ Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
+ Recruiting and training local personnel
+ Building a raw material supply chain form Cantral region suppliers
+ Launching campaigns for each store
- Time: This project must completed at least 12 month before open store
- Cost: This project must exceed VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening
