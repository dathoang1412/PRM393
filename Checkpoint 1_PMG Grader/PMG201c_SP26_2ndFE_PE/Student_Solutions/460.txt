Request 1
      1. Project name: Coi Cafe flagship stores.
      2. Reason: Coi Cafe is a famous coffee brand for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model( using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers). 
       Purpose: Coi's management want to expand their business, open 1 flagship store in each Central provinces. 
     3.  Scope: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and lauching campaigns for each store.
        Time: 12 months
        Cost/budget: VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
        Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.



Request 2:
     Five main cost/budget items:
         1. Name: Surveying and premises.
             Description: Run ads on website or social media to make surveying and selecting premises.
             How to estimate: Based on which website and social media run ads, some of them is free.
             Person in charged: HR and Marketing team  

         2. Name: Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand.
             Description: Amount of money to desgin and construct interiors, make them to be consistent with the brand.    
             How to estimate: Based on the difficulity of designing and constructing interiors. 
             Person in charged: Director

         3. Name: recruiting and traning local personnel.
             Description: The cost of recruiting and training local personnel.
             How to estimate: The cost based on the time that they use to recruit and trainning local personnel. 
             Person in charged: HR team

         4. Name: Building a raw material supply chain

Request 3: Three project risks
      1. Name: Run out of budget
           Description: Some of stores use more than VND 1.7 billion during 12 months so the total budget isn't enough.
           Impact: high
           Mitigation: Change the method of work to reduce the use of money like don't lauch huge campaigns.

      2. Name: The service isn't good
           Description: Some staffs don't behave well so customers isn't happy with that and they dislike brand.
          Impact: high
          Mitigation: Should have some courses to training staffs and make surveys for customers feedback.
          Contingency: Say sorry to customers and give them voucher for next time.

      3. Name: Don't reach the break-even point
          Description: Store don't reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
         Impact: Medium
         Mitigation: They should make a suitable timeline of their work to reach on time.
          Contingency: They should speed up their work as much as possible.
