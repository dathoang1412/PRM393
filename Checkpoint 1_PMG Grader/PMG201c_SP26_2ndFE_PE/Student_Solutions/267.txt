Request 1: Overview
1. Project name: Coi's Traditional Coffee Value_The traditional Vietnamese coffee value of Coi Cafe.

2. Justifications:
The "Coi's Traditional Coffee" is the campaign of Coi Cafe organize. Coi Cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee brand wwith 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh city. Specially, Coi Cafe famous for nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model (using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers).
This project is organized because Coi Cafe want to improve people of 5 Central provinces know the brand name of Cafe, show more value of the brand and quailty of Coi Cafe drink and coffee. Besides that, Coi Cafe want to build a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, design and construct interiors consistent with the brand. 
The project aims to reach people into 5 Central provineces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, BinhDinh). Purpose is surveying and selecting premises, designing and consstructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain form Central region suppliers. Especially, the project have flagship store in each province within 12 months. That will attract the local population join the campaign.
