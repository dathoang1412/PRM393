Request 1 
Project Name:
 Coi Cafe Expansion to Central Vietnam
Purpose / Justification:
 This project focuses on opening five new Coi Cafe stores in Central Vietnam within one year. The expansion targets key provinces including Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, and Binh Dinh.
The main objectives are to expand the company’s market coverage, respond to increasing coffee consumption in tourist areas, and develop a more flexible supply system using local partners. It also helps the company reduce dependence on current markets and improve long-term profitability.
Success Criteria:
Open all 5 stores within 12 months
Reach break-even point within 8 months
Maintain consistent service and product quality
Keep total cost under VND 8.5 billion
Stakeholders:
 Board of Directors, Project Manager, HR, Marketing, Design Team, Suppliers, Customers
Constraints:
Scope: Only 5 stores
Time: 12 months
Cost: 8.5 billion VND
Quality: Must follow brand standards

Risk 1: Project Delay
 Construction or setup may take longer than expected.
 Impact: Delay schedule, increase cost
 Mitigation: Careful planning and monitoring
 Contingency: Add extra time buffer
Risk 2: Weak Customer Demand
 Stores may not attract enough customers.
 Impact: Low revenue
 Mitigation: Strong marketing and location analysis
 Contingency: Adjust promotion strategy
Risk 3: Supplier Risk
 Suppliers may not deliver on time.
 Impact: Product shortage
 Mitigation: Multiple suppliers
 Contingency: Backup sourcing
