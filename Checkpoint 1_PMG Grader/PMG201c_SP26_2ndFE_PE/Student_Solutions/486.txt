Request 1:
1.1. Project name: Coi Cafe's Store Expansion Strategy into 5 Central Provinces Project

1.2. Justifications: The projects aims to expand Coi Cafe with larger scalability, more business impact on 5 Central provinces, including Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh. Besides, Coi Cafe's management intends to implement 1 flagship store in each province for the first 12 months.

1.3. Project constraints:
1.3.1 Scope:
1.3.1.1 There are 5-targeted provinces in the Central of Vietnam.
1.3.1.2 The project intends to implement 1 flagship store in each province
1.3.1.3 Each campaign should be launched with the activities of survey analysis, personnel management, constructing activity, supply chain planning

1.3.2 Time:
1.3.2.1 5-targeted provinces should have their own one flagship within 12 months after launching project
1.3.2.2 Each store must achieve its break-even point within 8 months after the implementation.

1.3.3 Cost/Budget:
1.3.3.1 8.5 billion VND, approximately 1.7 billion for each store from equity and commercial bank loans.

1.3.4 Quality:
1.3.4.1 Each store should be planned and be implemented with consistent and statble quality, as well as maintaining the core value of Coi Cafe - a traditional drink store, sustainable business model

Request 3: 
3.1 Human resources attrition:
3.1.1 Name: Human resources attrition
3.1.2 Description: There should be a attrition in the human resources while implementing the project.
3.1.3 Possible impacts: Some operation activities may be paused due to lack of staffs, managers. And, perhaps, there should be struggled with finding  a new alternative human resource to fill in.
3.1.4 Relevant risk response plans:
3.1.4.1 Mitigation: 
3.1.4.1.1 There should be well documented about all of the tasks, performance to prepare for any occurence of staff attrition.
3.1.4.1.2 Creating a friendly and well-beneficial working environment
3.1.4.2 Contingency:
3.1.4.2.1 Offer new benefits to keep them work continously
3.1.4.2.2 Give new opportunities with different roles or tasks

3.2 Overdue deadline for goods express service
3.2.1 Name: Overdue deadline for goods express service
3.2.2 Description: During the implementation activities in each store, there should a potential risk that goods express service may be lacked or missed due to weather reason, traffic context
3.2.3 Possible impacts: There would be not enough goods to prepare for business day.
3.2.4 Relevant risk response plans:
3.2.4.1 Mitigation: 
3.2.4.1.1 Create a commitment contract with the service representative to avoid any occurrance of being overdue.
3.2.4.1.2 Always have an/many alternative source(s) to be well available 
3.2.4.2 Contingency: Change to an alternative provider

3.3 Construction downgrade due to time
3.3.1 Name: Construction downgrade due to time
3.3.2 Description: Day by day, there is potential risk that each interior could be downgraded or low-level quality because of weather, environment (flood, storm, etc) so we should prepare a solution for this risk.
3.3.3 Possible impacts: any store operation could be paused partially or immediately. Besides, each store should be well-prepared in financial resouces to fix this risk.
3.3.4 Relevant risk response plans:
3.3.4.1 Mitigation: The operation of designing and architecture should be well-planned and implemented. And, the cost to invest into each part of the construction should be considered with adequate quality before running.
3.3.4.2 Contingency: Always have a construction support service to fix immediately or in a close period of time.
