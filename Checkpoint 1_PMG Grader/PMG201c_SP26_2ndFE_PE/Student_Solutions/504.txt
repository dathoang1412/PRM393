Request 1 (20%) – Project Charter
Project Name:
Coi Cafe Subscription & Community Space Project – Central Vietnam 2026

Justification (Purpose & Reasons):
This project introduces a subscription-based coffee model combined with community spaces, targeting young professionals and students in Central Vietnam. Instead of relying on traditional per-transaction sales, Coi Cafe will offer monthly membership packages that provide customers with daily beverages, workspace access, and exclusive benefits.
The purpose of this strategy is to:
Generate stable and predictable revenue streams through subscriptions
Increase customer retention and loyalty
Transform stores into community hubs rather than just coffee shops
Differentiate from competitors by offering added value beyond beverages
This model aligns with evolving consumer behavior, where customers seek both convenience and social experiences in a single location.
Project Constraints
Scope:
Launch 2 subscription-based café locations in major cities (Da Nang, Hue)
Develop membership system (app or card-based)
Design café spaces to support working, studying, and small events
Offer subscription packages (daily coffee, workspace access, combo deals)
Time:
Total duration: 12 months
Concept & system development: 4 months
Store setup: 5 months
Pilot testing & optimization: 3 months
Break-even target: within 7–9 months
Cost/Budget:
Total budget: VND 8.5 billion
Allocation:
Store setup & design: ~4.5 billion
Membership system & app: ~1 billion
Marketing & launch campaigns: ~2 billion
Contingency fund: ~1 billion
Funding from internal investment and strategic partners
Quality:
Ensure high-quality beverages and comfortable workspace environment
Maintain stable internet, seating, and service quality
Deliver seamless membership experience (easy access, flexible usage)
Customer satisfaction target ≥ 90%

Request 3 (30%) – Risk Management
Risk 1: Low Subscription Adoption
Description: Customers may hesitate to pay upfront for membership
Impact: Low recurring revenue
Mitigation: Offer trial packages and flexible pricing
Contingency: Shift focus to hybrid model (subscription + pay-per-use)
Risk 2: Overcrowding During Peak Hours
Description: Too many members using space at the same time
Impact: Poor customer experience
Mitigation: Limit membership slots and implement booking system
Contingency: Expand seating or adjust pricing tiers
Risk 3: High Operational Costs
Description: Maintaining workspace (electricity, internet, space) is costly
Impact: Reduced profitability
Mitigation: Optimize resource usage and pricing strategy
Contingency: Adjust subscription fees or reduce included benefits
