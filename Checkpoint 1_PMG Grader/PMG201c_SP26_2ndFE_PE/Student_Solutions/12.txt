Request 1:

1. Project name:

- Coi Cafe's expansion project 

2. Justification

- The purpose of this project is to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).

- Rreasons to implement this project are to have 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.

3.

- Project constraints: 

- In terms of scope: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Centrail region suppliers, launching campaigns for each store.

- Time: Within 12 months.

- Cost/Budget: Total: 8.5 Billion VND (about 1.7 Billion VND per store).

- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.




Request 2:

- Five main cost/budget items: Coffee Machines; Tote Bags; Cups, Coffees and Beverages; Coffee Stores.


+ Coffee Machines: This is the thing that uses to make coffee in Coi Cafe stores. To estimate this, we put the coffee machines inside the bar, the place that make beverages. The person in charge of these Coffee Machines are employees, manager. They gonna use to make coffee for customers, if the machine broke, they will be the first one to in charge. 

+ Tote Bags: Tote bags are put out for customers to buy. These are the merchandises that are parts of the project. To estimate this, first we design the bags,we produce it and sell inside the stores. The person in charge is the manager. Manager is the one who put these merchandises out the for customers. 

+ Cups: Just like Tote Bags, Cups are the merchandises too. To estimate this, we still need to design the cup, choose the good materials, produce and sell. The person in charge is the manager.

+ Coffees and Beverages: These are the main things about coffee stores. A wonderful coffee is gonna make customers to come back more times. And choices of beverages are for the customers who don't drink coffee or they need to have more choices. The person in charge is the whole store. If the beverages is bad. The whole store is gonna have bad rate. If the beverages is good. The whole store will have good rate. 

+ Coffee Stores: The location of the store is really important. If it had a great view, customers will love the view, and come back more time because of it. How to estimate is to find the good location which have big roads, lots of places to sit. The person who in charge is the owner, if he/she choose the place to build up a coffee store that is great, he/she will have profit.
