1. Proect Name:
Coi Cafe Coastal Lifestyle Expansion Project 2027
2. Justifications (Purpose & Reasons):
-Expand Coi Cafe into high-potential coastal markets in Central Vietnam (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh)
-Leverage the rapid growth of coastal tourism and lifestyle trends
-Strengthen Coi Cafe’s positioning as a culturally authentic Vietnamese coffee brand
-Increase market share and long-term profitability
-Enhance sustainability by integrating local sourcing and eco-friendly operations
The project is led by the Market Development Director as PM, coordinating with the interior design team, HR team, and marketing team. The total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans. Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
3. Project Constraints:
Scope:
-Open 5 flagship stores (one in each target province)
-Key activities include: site selection, lease negotiation, store design, construction, staff recruitment & training, supply chain setup, and marketing campaigns
Time:
-Total duration: 12 months
-Break-even target: within 8 months after store opening
Cost/Budget:
-Total investment: VND 8.5 billion
-Budget allocation averages VND 1.7 billion per store
Quality:
-Maintain Coi Cafe’s nostalgic Vietnamese design identity
-Ensure consistent service quality across all stores
-Apply sustainable business practices (eco-friendly materials, ethical sourcing)
