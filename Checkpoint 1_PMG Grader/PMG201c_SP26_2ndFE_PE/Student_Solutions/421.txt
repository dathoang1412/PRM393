Request 1:

Project name: Coi Cafe central provinces expansion project

Justification: The project primary purpose is to expnase and spread out to 5 central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) to increase the market size and also increase the brand identification to Vietnamese by its famous nostalgic shop house space and traditional drink menu. Another side purpose is to make use of the local material and culture to expand the brand diversity and strengthen the Vienamese traditional aspect of the brand

Constraint:

-Scope: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw mterial supply chain frrom Central region suppliers, and launching campaings for each store
-Time: 12 months
-Cost/Budget: VND 8.5 billion
-Quality: have a fully analyzed statistic from the survey and design an interiors consistent with the brand. Fully utilize local supply material and human resource.Furthermore, enhance brand strength by enrich traditional culture by each province culture

Request 3:

Risk 1:
Title: Varity of target province
Description: The increasing rate of low adaptability and possibility to meet to each province culture and local customer interest beacause of the diversity in culture of each province (which is really contrast within the 5 targeted provinces )
Scope: Little impact on the scope of the project
Time: Major impact, high chance of increasing the total project schedule because of the increasing in market research and survey time
Cost: Moderate impact, increasing in time of project schedule will follow up with the increasing in operation cost
Quality: High impact, adjusting product attribute to fit the interest and need of multiple local customer at once is very risky.
Mitigation: Dividing the scope of project into multi phase for a certain group of province at once
Contingency: Ready to shift the resource and invest to the positive result province

Risk 2: 
Title: Quality affected by the new raw material supply source
Description: The risk of trying new material supply from central region to cut down shipping cost and fit local culture have a high chance of decreasing in quality of product.
Scope: Little impact on scope
Time: Moderate impact, high chance of increasing time to find and adjust new source of supply to meet the traditional quality of product
Budget: High impact, increasing in time make the operation cost increase and also the cost for testing new supply
Quality: High impact, high rate of change and decrase in product quality
Mitigation: Taking local expert advise in coffee material
Contingency: Prepare plan to switch back to the tradition supply source

Risk 3: Budet overrun
Description: Infrastructure and operation cost in each province is contrast can make the budget increase
Scope: Little impact
Time: Little impact
Budget: High impact
Quality: Little impact
Mitigation: Create a careful and proper budget estimations
Contingency: Set up contingency budget to back up
