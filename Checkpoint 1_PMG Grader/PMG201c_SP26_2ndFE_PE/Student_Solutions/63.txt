Project Name: Coffee store and promotion campaign project

Purpose and reasons: The project aims to potential of coffee, a traditional drink in VietNam.Many place do not have coffee store. This project have create more store brand and make coffee become famous drink in VietNam

Project constraints:
Scope:Surveying and selecting premises , designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand , recruiting and training local personnel , building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store.
Each store must reach the break even point within 8 months after opening.

Time:12 months/1 store

Cost/Budget:8.5 billionVND(approximately 1.7 billion/store VND) from equity and commercial bank loans.

Quality: Each store reach the break even pont within 8 months after opening. The project is led by the Market Development Director as PM , coordinating with the interior design team, HR team, marketing team.
