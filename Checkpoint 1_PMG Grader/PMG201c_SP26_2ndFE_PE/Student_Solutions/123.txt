Request 1: 
Project name: Coi Cafe Management Project
Detailed description: Coi Cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, famous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model (using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers). Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months. The project includes: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store
Purpose: The purpose to implement this project is to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months
Project constraints
- Scope: 
+ Surveying and selecting premises
+ Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
+ Recruiting and training local personnel 
+ Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
+ Launching campaigns for each store
- Time: 12 months
- Cost/ Budget: VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening
