Request 3
Identify three project risks
1. Risk title is Scope Creep. This risk occur when stakeholder change very much request in the project. This risk is high impact because it can affect to cost and time and quality of project. Time is after project start. Risk response plan is the input request must be control and verify before allowed to apply in project.
2. Risk title is Key Employee leave in project team but do not notificate for team. This risk very high impact, it can affect to time and cost of project because key employee have the important position in develop team so team project will waste a lot of time to training another employee when this employee do not work with team. Time is after project start. Risk repsonse plan is team need work in pair to share the knowledge among another project team member and backup the important data of project, do not get one person keep the important data.
3. Risk title is Third-Party stop provide services. This risk low impact, it can affect to the time of project. Time is after project start. Risk response plan is find another service provider or self host services important of project.
