Request 3: Define at least 5 QA/QC Activities
1. Market and Location Review (QA):
At the beginning, the team reviews customer traffic, competitors, rental prices, visibility, accessibility, and customer habits in each province. This reduces the risk of choosing locations with weak business potential.

2. Budget Review and Cost Control (QA):
The project manager and finance team compare estimated costs with the total budget of VND 8.5 billion, or about VND 1.7 billion per store. This helps prevent overspending on construction, equipment, rental deposits, recruitment, and marketing.

3. Interior Design Approval (QA):
Before construction starts, the design team and management review the layout, furniture, lighting, decoration, and customer space. This ensures each store still reflects Coi Cafe’s nostalgic Vietnamese coffee concept.

4. Construction Quality Inspection (QC):
During construction, the team checks material quality, finishing work, electrical systems, furniture installation, equipment setup, and safety conditions. This helps detect problems early and reduce rework before opening.

5. Supplier Quality Evaluation (QC):
Before confirming suppliers, the team checks coffee sample quality, delivery reliability, price stability, and supplier capacity. This is important because unstable supply can affect drink quality and daily operations.

6. Staff Training Assessment (QC):
After training, employees are tested on drink preparation, customer service, POS usage, hygiene standards, and basic procedures. This ensures staff are ready to serve customers properly.

7. Soft Opening Evaluation (QC):
Before official launch, each store runs a short trial operation with limited customers. The team observes service speed, drink quality, equipment performance, staff coordination, and customer feedback, then fixes issues before grand opening.
