2)
Request 3:
Risk 1: Low customer foot traffic
+ Description: Fewer visitors than expected due to location or bad weather
+ Possible impacts: Cost
+ Mitigation plan: Increase digital advertising 2 days before the launch
+ Contingency plan: Distribute flyers in high-traffic areas nearby to pull in costumers
 
Risk 2: High product shrinkage
+ Description: Lack or damage of products exceeding 2% limit
+ Possible impacts: Quality (loss of stocks) and cost (financial loss)
+ Mitigation plan: Train staff on loss prevention and secure the display overnight
+ Contingency plan: Conduct daily stock counts to detect and address loss immediately

Risk 3: Staff shortage
+ Description: Staff is absent due to illness or busy
+ Possible impacts: Time (delayed serving) and quality (poor customer service)
+ Mitigation plan: Put advertising for more staff members
+ Contingency plan: The shop owner or managers will cover roles if necessary


Request 4:
- Project roles:
1. Project Manager: Oversee budget and quality control
2. Marketing team: Manages VND 8.5 billion for commerical loans
3. HR team: Manages the store in each province
