Project name: Implement an expansion Coi Cafe project

Project constraints:
Scope:This project includes: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing  interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and traning local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and lauching campaigns for each store and each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening
Time: The project have duration limited is 12 months for implement stores in 5 Central provinces with 1 flagship store in each provinces
Cost/budget: Project has total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximate VND 1.& billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.
Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
