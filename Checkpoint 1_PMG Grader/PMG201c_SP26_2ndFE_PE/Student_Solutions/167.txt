Request 1

Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project

Justofications:
Purpose: The project purpose is develop "Coi Cafe" brand and bring "Coi Cafe" to new marketplace 
Reasons: To implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces, with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months

Project constrains:
_Scope constraints: the project includes surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store
_Time constains: 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months. 
_Budget constrains: The total budget of project is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
_Quality constrains: The project quality is accepted if each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening
