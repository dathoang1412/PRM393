Request 1:
1.1 Project name: Coi Cafe Cental Provinces Expansion
1.2 Justification: Expanse Coi Cafe into 5 Cental provinces, provide coffee in nostalgic space and traditional drink that using friendly enviromental cup and materials from Cental region suppliers. Providing job opportunity for local area as employees and Cental region suppliers.
1.3 Project constraints:
- Scope:
+ Expansing into 5 Cental provinces
+ One flagship store in each province
+ Building a raw material supply chain form Cental region suppliers
+ Launching campaigns for each store
+ Break-event point within 8 months after opening
- Time: 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months
- Budget: VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
- Quality:
+ Quality survey provide useful data 
+ Future store maintain their unique like nostalgic space, traditional drink, sustainable business model
+ Consistent designing and constructing interiors
+ Break-event point within 8 months after opening
+ Good training employees

Request 2:
- Surveying and selecting premises: Surveying local people face to face or online through website or link. Must be have good team in term of understanding what to ask and where to ask. Surveying total 10000 people in all platform. Around 3% of total budget each store to operate a team.
- Designing and constructing. Around 40% budget each store to rent place, designing and contrusting place that has nostalgic space and beautiful design and interiors
- Recruiting and training local personnel so that can operate store smoothly without owner. Around 10% of budget each store to train, hire experience trainners and non experience employees. 
- Building a raw material supply chain from Cental region suppliers that have certificates and good background. Around 20% of budget of each store to suppliers provide material consistenly
- Launching campaigns for people to introduce, marketing store. About 7% budget each store for a campaign that has capacity of 1000 people, rent place, announcement fee.
- Marketing through KOLs, online platform, poster,.... About 20% of total budget each store to rent KOLs, marketing online platform, print poster about a store or event, operate a team, ...

Request 3:
Three risks are:
- Over budget: Using budget not correctly or have additional fees that not calculate at start. Impact: Can be affect to original plan that reduce Cental provinces or quality of cheap material, poor-training employee that may affect customer experience and operation of store. Wasting more time to create alternative way to solve the problem. Response plan: Having reserve budget to use when it needed, planning alternative plan for all situtiation before excution to resolve immediately when it happend.
- Task complete not on time. Some work take time longer than expected plan or unexpected accident happen. Impact: It will break the original timeline plan, may effect the budget to keep the store on time to open, operate. Response plan: Having reserve plan when it happend, may reduce low impact task to keep on plan. Use reserve fees to hire people to complete task. 
- Conflict of each team when coordinate. Maybe there are a fight or a unacceptance working behavior,... can create unhealthy working enviroment. Impact: Using more time to resolve conflict and may need high level worker attendance to resolve and punish. The problem may resolve but the relationship between still complex. This reduce the effective of coordinating. Response plans: Having clearly tasks and acceptance of each team at planning phase. Providing clearly punishment of each situtation.

Request 4:
4.1 Three project roles are Market Development Director as PM, interior design team, marketing team
4.2 Ten tasks are: Surveying and selecting premises, choose a place, review place, design, contruct, approval, recruiting employee, training employee, operate store, marketing 
4.3 
- Surveying and selecting. PM is responsible for review question, data. HR team and marketing team are responsible for choosing question and doing surevey.
- Choose a place. Interior design team is responsible for choosing suitable place for their brand vibe and possibly design beautiful.
- Review place. PM is responsible for final desicion to choose a place for a store.
- Design. Interior design team is responsible to design an overview of the store include design, interior,...
- Construct. Interior design team is responsible for coordinate with buiding team to build the place that almost look the same as design.
- Approval place. PM is responsible for review the store and acceptance.
- Recruiting employee. HR team, marketing team is responsible for announce to people to know.
- Training employee. Trainning employees is reponsible for trainning newbie employee
- Operate store. Store manager is responsible for the store that operate smoothly with consistently income
- Marketing. Marketing is responsible for choosing content and where, when to market
