Request 1 : 
1. Project name : Coi Cafe Vietnamese Campaign
2. Justifications : 
 - Purpose : Expand into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai and Binh Dinh) with 1 flagship store in each province
 - Reason : Increase using reusable cups, purchasing coffee diractly from Dak Lak farmers and promotion Vietnamese coffee around Viet Nam.
3. Project constraints :
- In terms of scope : 
  - Surveying and training local personnel
  - Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
  - Launching campaigns to each store
- Time : 
  - Strictly 12 months
- Cost/Budget : 
  - Total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion each store)
  - Each store must reach the brack-even point 8 months after opening
- Quality : 
  - Using reusable cups
  - Purchasing coffee from Dak Lak farmers
  - Using traditional drink of Viet Nam.

Request 2 : 
-Six cost/budget items : 
1.  Setup Store Cost
 - Description : The cost pay for setup, decoration for the store
 - Estimation : VND 400 million 
 - Estimate method : Due to the decoration in the market (table, chair, the decoration for coffee store...)
 - Person in charged : Design Team
2. Training Cost and Salary : 
 - Description : The cost for training employees
 - Estimation : VND 100  to 200 million
 - Estimate method : 5 - 7 employees, each person has salary from VND 10 to 15 million
 - Person in charged : HR Team
3. Purchasing inventory : 
 - Description : The cost pay for coffee, cups and another drinks.
 - Estimation : VND 400 - 500 million
 - Estimate method : The coffee is about VND 250 - 300 million, the cups is about VND 100 million and anothers drink is about VND 100 million
 - Person in charge : Project Manager
4. Marketing Cost : 
 - Description : The marketing and promotion cost
 - Estimation : VND 200 million
 - Estimate method : Base on the marketing price at all social media and local store.
 - Person in charged : Marketing Team
5. Operating processing : 
 - Description : The cost pay for operating store
 - Estimation : VND 100 million
 - Estimate method : Base on the electrics,water and  the place fee in the market
 - Person in charged : Project Manager
6. Contingency Cost : 
 - Description : The cost for all risk when risk happen
 - Estimation : VND 200 million
 - Estimate method : Base on the risk of all cost
 - Person in charged : Project Manager

Request 3 : 
 Three project risks : 
 1. Beak-even Risk : 
  - Description : Can't reach break-even point at the end of Month 8 after opening the campaign
  - Possible Impact : 
   - In term of scope : Can't get profit and equity
   - Time : Reach break-even at the end of Month 10
   - Cost : Not reach to profit at the end of project
   - Mitigation : Marketing early before the opening store for 2 weeks, have a evens when opening 
  - Contigency : Increase marketing cost, decrease set-up cost.
 2. Inventory Risk : 
  - Description : Not enough inventory ( reusable cups, coffee...)
  - Possible Impact :  
   - In term of scope : Loss profit and delay the project finish.
   - Time : Increase from 12 months to 15 months
   - Cost : Decrease the profit and equity of the store
    - Mitigation : Research more the suppliers in Dak Lak
    - Contigency : Increase the cost of purchasing inventory
 3. The set-up cost Risk
  - Description : The set-up cost is more than the budget
  - Possible Impact : 
   - In term of scope : Increase the total budget 
   - Time : Delay the time of opening store.
   - Cost : The set-up cost increase, make the total budget increase
  - Relevant Risk : 
   - Mitigation : Research the decoration market carefully and don't buy unecessary items.
   - Contingency : Decrease the set-up cost if the cost increase of increase the set-up cost and decrease the training cost.
