Project name: 

Coi Cafe the Vietnamese coffee from Dak Lak with ambitious expansion.

Justification:

Currently, Coi Cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. They known for not only nostalgic space but also sustainable business model that is using reusable cups. 
Beside that, many people love Dak Lak coffee and their "green mission" friendly to our planet. They recognize the potention of their brand, therefore Coi's founder willing to opening more and more stores, focus into 5 Central provinces of the middel of VietNam. Especially 1 flagship store in each province.

Project constraints:

Scope: surveying, selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain fron Centrl region suplliers, and launching campaigns for each store.

Time: 1 flagship store for each province within 12 months (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh)

Budget: The total budget is 8.5 billion VND 
(approximately 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.

Quality:  Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Three project risks 

1: The schedule too tight, it's hard to complete on time 
Possible: Low
Impact: High
Mitigation plan: extend the shedule, recruit more suitable position if need 
Contingency plan: always follow team's processing, give a KPI and reward to achieve

2: The Project Manager have a poor skill management
Possible: Low
Impact: High
Mitigation plan: contribute and recommend PM to learn the skill
Contingency plan: recruit a assistant who has a high skills management, can support PM

3: Overbudget, commercial bank loans strictly 
Possible: Low
Impact: Low
Mitigation plan: minimum the cost as possible as could, evaluate the cost out of scope.
Contingency plan: have a extra backup budget 
(about 10%) of total
