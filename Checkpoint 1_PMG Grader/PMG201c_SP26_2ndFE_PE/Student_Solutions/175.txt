1. Project name: Coi Cafe
2. Justifications(purpose & reasons to implement this project)
- Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).
- Coi Cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, farmous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model(using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers).
3. Project contraints
- In terms of Scope: The project includes surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and lauching campaigns for each store.
- Time: 1 flagship store in each province withing 12 months. Total 5 province is 60 months.
- Cost/Budget: The total budget is VND 8.5 billion(approximately VND 1.7 billion/store).
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
