   Time :

    - Each store must reach the break-even point             within 8 months after opening 

   Cost/Budget : 
    - The total budget is VND 8.5 billion              (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store).
 
   Quality : 
    - The project is led by the Market Development            Director as PM, coordinating with the interior              design team, HR team and marketing team.
