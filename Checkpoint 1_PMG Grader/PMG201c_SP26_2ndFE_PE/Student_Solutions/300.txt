Request 1
1. Project name:
Coi Cafe Central Region Expansion Project

2. Justifications:
Purpose: Opent 5 flagship stores in 5 provinces in Central of Vietnam (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh). We must do this by 12 months.

Reasons: To bring the successful nostalgic space, traditional drink, and green business to a new market and get more revenue.

3. Project constraints:
Scope: Make exactly 5 stores in 5 provinces. We need to find locations, build interior, hire and train local staff, find local suppliers, and run marketing.

Time: Finish all works in 12 months.

Cost/budget: Total budget is 8.5 billion VND (around
1.7 billion VND per store). The money come from company and bank loan.

Quality: Store design must match the brand. We must use sustainable model (reusable cup, direct buy). Each store need to reach break-even point in 8 months.       


Request 2
1. Renting Premises

Desciption: Find and rent place for 5 stories in 5 provinces.

Estimation: VND 1,500,000,000

Way to estimate: Parametric estimating (multiply average rent price per square meter with the store area).

Percon in charge: Project Manager

2. Interior Construction

Description: Design and buld interior for 5 stores to match the brand.

Estimation: VND 4,000,000,000 

Way to estimate: Analogous estimating (use the old cost when bulding stores in Hanoi and HCM city).

Person in charge: Interior Design Team Lead

3. Equipment adn Furniture

Description: Buy coffee machine, reusable cup, and nostalgic furniture

Estimation: VND 1,500,000,000

Way to estimate: Bottom-up estimating (sum the price quotes from suppliers for each item).

Person in charge: Project Manager

4. Marketing Campaigns

Description: Run marketing campaign for new stores in 5 provinces.

Estimate: VND 500,000.000

Way to estimate: Expert judgment (Marketing team guess based on old successful campaigns).

Person in charge: Marketing Team Lead

5. HR Recruitment and Training

Description: Hire and train local staffs for 5 locations.

Estimation: 250,000,000 VND

Way to estimate: Parametric estimate (number of employees will be multiplied by the typical training cost per person).

Person in charge: HR Team Lead


Request 3

1. Risk name: Late Premises Handover

Description: Finding and choosing the store location take more time because there are not many good places in Central provinces.

Possible impacts: (Time) Make the 12 month schedule late. (Cost) Increase project management cost.

Risk response plans:

Mitigation: Start to find locations right now and use local real estate agents.

Contingency: Do the interior design faster to save time.

2. Risk name: Supply Chain Problem 

Description: The project cannot find raw material suppliers in Central region that follow sustainable rules.

Possible impacts: Cannot make traditional drinks or keep the green business model.

Risk response plans:

Mitigation: Find and check many Central suppliers early in the project.

Contingency: Buy materials from old Dak Lak farmers or Hanoi/HCm city suppliers for a short time until we find local suppliers.

3. Risk name: Low Financial Revenue

Description: New stores do not have enogh custiomers to reach break-even point in 8 months.

Possible impacts: Make the company lose money and hard to pay the bank loan.

Risk response plans:

Mitigation: Do market research carefully and run very strong marketing campaign before opening. 

Contingency: Give big discount for local people and cut down daily running cost.
