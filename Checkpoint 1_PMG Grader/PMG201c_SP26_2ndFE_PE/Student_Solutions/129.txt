Request 1:
1. Project Name:
Coi Cafe Regional Flagship Store Implementation Project

2. Justifications:
- Purpose:
The purpose of this project is to implement 5 flagship stores in the Central region and support Coi Cafe’s controlled regional expansion. The project turns the company’s growth decision into clear stores, teams, suppliers, and campaigns.

- Reason:
Coi Cafe has a distinctive brand because it combines Vietnamese coffee, nostalgic design, sustainable practices, and direct farmer sourcing. Opening stores in the Central region helps the company reach new customers, improve brand coverage, and create more revenue opportunities.

3. Project Constraints:
- Scope:
The project includes selecting premises, designing and building interiors, hiring and training local staff, developing Central supplier sources, and launching store campaigns.
- Time:
The full project must be completed within 12 months. Each store must reach break-even within 8 months after opening.
- Cost/Budget:
The total budget is VND 8.5 billion and must cover all 5 flagship stores.
- Quality:
Store space, drink menu, service process, staff training, and sustainable image must be consistent with Coi Cafe’s current standard.
