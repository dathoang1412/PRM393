Request 1:
- Project name: Koi Cafe
- Purpose: Implement an expansion for this coffee brand into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province.
- Scope:
+ Surveying and selecting premises
+ Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
+ Recruiting and training local personnel
+ Buildding a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
+ Lauching campaigns for each store.
- Time: within 12 months
- Cost/budget: total is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
- Quality: Each store much reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.


Request 2: 5 budget items:
1. Surveys:
- Description: serveying the local culture and demands. They take place in extistence stores and on social media like facebook, tiktok,...
- Estimation: VND 0.1 billion/store
- How to estimate: base on salary of PIC and forms, goods and gifts,..
- PIC: Marketing team
2. Interior design and construction:
- Description: Design and construction for interior are consistent with the brand.
- Estimation: VND 0.1 billion for design and VND 0.8 billion for construction each store
- How to estimate: base on design and construction price estimation of each region.
- PIC: interior design team
3. Recruiting and training local personnel:
- Description: Recruiting local employee and holding internal trainning for personnel.
- Estimation: VND 0.1 billion/store
- How to estimation: base on previous recruitign and training cost.
- PIC: HR team
4. Raw material supply chain:
- Description: Building  a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, which reduces material cost and keeps product quality consistence.
- Estimation: VND: 0.2 billion/store
- How to estimate: depends on region suppliers.
- PIC: Market Development Director (PM)
5. Campaigns for each store:
- Description: Campaigns include opening, sale days, holidays,... which improves store identity and sales.
- Estimation: 0.3 billion/store
- How to estimation: base on previous campains
- PIC: PM and marketing team

Request 3:
1. Risk 1: Design and constructing interior is out of budget
- Description: the budget in reality may reach out of calculation.
- Impacts: 
+ Scope: budget shortage prevent the opening progress of store, thus effecting other tasks
+ Time: delay the opening time
+ Cost: may cause labor price go further
- Response plans: use the emergency budget, change the plan/design for more economical price.

2. Risk 2: Raw materila supply chain is not completed
- Description: the Central region suppliers are not found or not established the supply chain.
- Impacts:
+ Scope/quality: if supply chain is not built, new store will face raw material shortage and effect daily activities.
+ Time: The opening of stores should be delayed or stopped.
+ Cost: because of raw material shortage, pre-sale product cost is rising, therefore running cost of each store is higer.
- Response plans: Change the drink menu for focusing on drinks which have local raw material; establish temporary supply chain from further region for raw materials,...

3. Risk 3: Employee shortage for recruitment
- Description: local employee is not enough for recruitment.
- Impacts:
+ Scope: without enough staffs, stores are not running efficiency, customer may have not fully service,..
+ Time: The opening of stores should be delayed or stopped.
+ Cost: rise traning cost for each staffs and recruitment
- Response plans: temporary move staffs from exists stores; training for staffs to improve efficiency.
