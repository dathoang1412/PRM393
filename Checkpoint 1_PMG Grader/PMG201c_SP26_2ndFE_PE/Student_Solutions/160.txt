Request 1 (20%) – Project Charter
Project Name:
Coi Cafe Central Market Penetration Project 2026
Justification (Purpose & Reasons)
This project is initiated to introduce Coi Cafe into five Central provinces: Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, and Binh Dinh. These markets are currently experiencing growth in urban lifestyle trends, especially among young consumers who prefer modern café environments.
Expanding into these regions will help Coi Cafe build a stronger nationwide footprint, attract new customer segments, and increase overall business resilience by reducing reliance on existing major cities. In addition, the project supports sustainable development by promoting local sourcing and efficient resource utilization.
Project Constraints
Scope:
 The project involves launching 5 new stores across selected provinces, ensuring all locations follow unified branding, standardized operations, and high service quality. It also includes staff hiring, training, and supplier coordination.
Time:
 The project must be completed within 12 months. Each store is expected to reach operational break-even within 8 months of launch.
Cost/Budget:
 The total allocated budget is VND 8.5 billion, averaging approximately VND 1.7 billion per store, financed through internal funds and external borrowing.
Quality:
 Each store must maintain consistent standards in design, product quality, customer service, and environmental practices. All employees must be trained before opening.

Request 3 (30%) – Risk Management
Risk 1: Construction Delays
Description: Delays due to contractor performance or external factors
Impact: Increased project duration and additional costs
Mitigation: Careful contractor selection and strict supervision
Contingency: Add buffer time and adjust scheduling

Risk 2: Insufficient Customer Demand
Description: New stores may not attract enough customers initially
Impact: Low revenue and delayed break-even
Mitigation: Conduct feasibility studies and target suitable locations
Contingency: Increase promotional campaigns and adjust pricing

Risk 3: Supply Disruptions
Description: Issues with supplier delivery or quality inconsistency
Impact: Operational interruptions and reduced service quality
Mitigation: Work with multiple suppliers and monitor performance
Contingency: Activate backup suppliers and maintain inventory buffer
