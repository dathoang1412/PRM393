RQ1:
1. Project Name
Coi Cafe Central Region Expansion Project
2. Justifications
Purpose: Coi Cafe wants to grow. We will open 5 new flagship stores in Central Vietnam.
Reasons: The brand is already famous in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. Now, we want to bring our traditional coffee and green business to 5 new provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh). This will help the company make more money.
3. Project Constraints
Scope: We must open exactly 5 stores. The work includes finding locations, building interiors, hiring local staff, finding local suppliers, and running marketing campaigns.
Time: The project must finish in exactly 12 months.
Cost/Budget: The total budget is VND 8.5 billion. This means we have about VND 1.7 billion for each store. We cannot spend more than this.
Quality: Each store must look nostalgic. They must sell our traditional menu and use eco-friendly cups. Also, every new store must break even within 8 months after opening.
RQ2:
5 Main Cost Items
1. Store rent and deposite
Description: Money to rent 5 places. We must pay deposit to the landlords first.
Estimation: 1.5 billion VND.
Way to estimate: PM will ask local agents for market price. They use analogous estimating from our old stores. Person in charge: PM.
2. Interior contruction and design
Description: Make the store look nostalgic. It needs traditional style. This take a lot of money.
Estimation: 3.5 billion VND.
Way to estimate: Bottom up estimation. The design team count all materials like wood and lights. Then they caculate total. Person in charge: Interior design team.
3. Coffee equipments and furnitures
Description: We buy coffee machines, fridge, and reusable cups.
Estimation: 2 billion VND.
Way to estimate: Get direct qoutes from sellers. We use parametric estimating (price of 1 machine x 5 stores). Person in charge: PM.
4. Hiring and tranining staffs
Description: Find local people. Teach them how to make our traditional menu.
Estimation: 0.5 billion VND.
Way to estimate: HR team check local basic salary. They estimate training cost based on hours needed. Person in charge: HR team.
5. Grand opening maketing
Description: Run ads when the stores open. We need many customers to reach break-even soon.
Estimation: 1 billion VND.
Way to estimate: Use historical data from old campaigns in Hanoi. They set a fixed budget per store. Person in charge: Marketing team.
RQ3:
3 Project Risks
1. Cannot find good locations
Description: We need 5 big spaces in nice streets. But maybe the good places are already taken by other coffee shops.
Possible Impacts:
Scope/Quality: The store might be smaller than we want.
Time: It takes many months to find, so the project is late.
Cost: We have to pay higher rent to get a good place.
Risk Response Plans:
Mitigation: Start searching right now. Hire local brokers to help us find fast.
Contingency: If we cannot find any place in month 3, we must accept a higher rent price or a smaller size.
2. Contruction is too slow
Description: The local workers might work very slow. Sometimes the weather in Central is bad and they have to stop working.
Possible Impacts:
Scope/Quality: The store looks ugly because they rush to finish it.
Time: We miss the 12 months deadline.
Cost: We must pay extra money for workers to do over-time.
Risk Response Plans:
Mitigation: Choose very famous builders. The PM must check their work every single week.
Contingency: If they are late, we hire another team to work at the night shift.
3. Fail to find local suppy
Description: We want to buy materials from Central suppliers to save money. But their coffee quality might be bad.
Possible Impacts:
Scope/Quality: The drinks do not taste like our traditional Coi Cafe.
Time: We waste a lot of time testing bad suppliers.
Cost: Shipping materials from far away will cost a lot of money.
Risk Response Plans:
Mitigation: Test the materials very early. Ask them for samples before we sign the contract.
Contingency: If all local suppliers are bad, we just ship coffee directly from our old farm in Dak Lak.
