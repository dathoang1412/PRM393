Request 3: 
Project Risk: 
1,  Low amount of customers. 
 - Description: Becase of the brand Coi Cafe is new with the local people, so the early opening time would be low amount of customers. 
 - Possibility: High
 - Impact: Medium. Break-even point would be delayed, staff hiring salary cost but no revenue.  
- Response: Mitigate.
- Strategy: Have a advertisement  campaign (social media, poster, sale off). Focus on the quality of the coffee and the customer experience. 

2. Out of coffee
- Description: Because  the coffee are purchased directly from Dak Lak Farmer so there is a possibility that some season in the year the coffee could not enough 
- Posibility : Low
- Impact: Medium . The quality of the coffee would be down, customers would be dissapointed if  the coffee out of stock. Take more time for waiting the coffee supply, and cost more budget for coffee sources. 
- Response : Transfer.
- Strategy: Buy and save a large amount of coffee beans. Find at least 5 sources of coffee from Dak Lak 

3. The risk of the flood in the middle of VietNam
- Desciption: The campaign is focus in Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh. So would be many disadvantages from the flood from the July to December every year.
 - Posibility : High 
 -Impact: High . The amount of customers would decrease, staff can not go to work because of the traffic and weather , The store would be close during the flood days, Building and funiture would be harm by the causes of the flood. 
 - Response: Accept. 
- Strategy: At first the store should be built on high place and using the high quality of building material to avoid least damage. The store should be closed during the hard weather days.
