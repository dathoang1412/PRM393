Request 1:
- Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project

- Justification:
    + The project is initiated because the company needs to expand the their market to 5 central provinces such as Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh. In the context of, their personal branding concept is for nostalgic space, traditional drink menu and resuable materials such as cups.
   + The project is taking a survey to collect the customer behavior, the feedback and the supplier statics from the target place that the company want to take part in. These analytics make a strong affect on the company decision on product price, concept store, marketing plan, etc,...

- Project constraint:
  + Scope: The project scope is including activities such as surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors of local store consistent with the brand, recruiting and performing tranining session for store staff, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers and lauching a campaigns for store in 5 Central provinces. Any extra activities is out of scoped unless formally approved
 + Time: The project timeline is fixed in 12 months. All planning, preparing interiors store concenpt, building pipeline for a raw material, launching campaign, creating training session for local pesonnel must be completed within this timline. Delays in critical activities may affect the overall project performance
 + Cost: The project budget is totall approximately VND 8.5 billion. Each store will have to finished the campaign, do marketing, and prepare equipment with the target amount of money is VND 1.7 billion. These cost will mainly come from the equity and commercial bank loans. The project manager need to controll the cost carefully and ensure all the deliverables must be finished within this allocated budget. Any overspending requires sponsor approval
  + Quality: The project have to meet the standard campaign launched such as reaching high attractive of the local customer, earned fast within 8 months to return loans to the banks, high recognition about the flagship store, and having a supply chain from Centrail provinces with optimize cost.
