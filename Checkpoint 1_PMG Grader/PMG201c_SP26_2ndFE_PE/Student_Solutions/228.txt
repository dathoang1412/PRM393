Project Charter:
Project name: GreenCoi Project
Detailed description:
- The project will be surveying and selecting premises, designing and contructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personel, buildig a raw material supply and launching campaigns for each newly opened store located in 5 Central provinces.
- Purpose: To impplement an expansion into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh). This project is in orderto bring the nostalgic space, tradition drinkmenu and sustainable model to the central part of Vietnam.
Project Constrains:
- Scope: 
  + Surveying and selecting premises
  + Designing and constructing interiors with the brand
  + Recruiting and training local personel
  + Building a raw material supply chain from Central Region suppliers
  + Launching campaigns for each store.
- Time: Within 12 months with 1 flagship store in each province
 - Cost/budget: VND 8.5 billion (approximate VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.
 - Quality: Each store much reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening

REQUEST 3:
Three project risks:
- Overly used budget
  +The cost of the project might surpassed VND 8.5 Biilion.
  + Possible impact: there will be not enoung money for latern items
  + Response plan: building a cost contingency and mitigating all low-needed item.
 - Long construsting time:
  + Central provinces are frequently under natural disaster, this can extend the constructing time
  + Possible impact: It takes more time to open a store and might affect to the break-even point.
  + Response plan: investigate all the quality of the places to make sure to construct and open in a perfect condition.
  - Low quality or unfamiliar supplies.
  + Local supplies are maybe diiferent than the original supply or the quality is not met
  + Posible impact: the product made from local supplies is not good as the originals, lower the quality of the products.
  + Response plan: Reduce the quanlity of local supplies in used and expand suppliers radius (Daklak).
