1.2.1. Purpose: implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) with 1 flagship store for each within 12 months

1.2.2. Reasons: To make Coi Cafe become more popular.

1.3.1. Constraints:
+ Resources: Design Team, HR Team, Marketing Team and lead by Market Development Director (Project manager)

1.3.2. Cost

1.3.3. Time:

+ Survey and select premises
+ Design and construct interiors consistent with the Coi Cafe Brand
+ Recruit and train new local employees. 
+ Build a raw material supply chain from Central region supplier.
+ Lauch campaigns for each store

REQUEST 3:
3.1. Fail to sign contract to supplier
+ Possible/Impact: Low/High
+ Description: Fail to sign contract to the supplier or the raw material is not suitable for the brand products
+ Impact: This may increase Time and cost or even can reduce the quality
+ Mitigation: Prepare a sustain amount of raw material to back up
+ Contingency: Find at least 2-3 suppliers in local area/Central region 

3.2. Lacks of employees
+ Possible/Impact: Low/High
+ Description: Can't find enough people for a store
+ Impact: Make the store's performance reduce
+ Mitigation: HR Team would work overtime
+ Contigency: Recuit Part-time workers

3.3. Over budget 
+ Possible/Impact: Medium/High
+ Description: All the cost or marketing plans are over budget of a store (over 1.7 billion VND)
+ Impact: increase the time for store to reach to break-event within 8 months
+ Mitigation: Inform to Coi Cafe owner about the situation. Find others store that under budget to back-up to this store.
+ Contigency: Reduce the store's scope and re-planing the marketing campaignss
