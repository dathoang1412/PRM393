Request 1: 
 1. Project Name: Coi Cafe Central Expansion Project (Coi Cafe CEP).

2. Justifications (purpose and reasons):

- To expand the brand to the Central region of Vietnam to grow the business.

- To bring traditional coffee and a nostalgic space to more customers.

3. Project Constraints:

- Scope: Open 1 flagship store in each of the 5 provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).

- Time: Must be completed within 12 months.

- Cost/Budget: Total budget is 8.5 billion VND (1.7 billion VND per store).

- Quality: Must reach break-even within 8 months after opening.

Request 3:
1. Delay in store construction

-Description: The stores are not built on time because of contractor problems.

-Possible impacts: Time (takes more than 12 months), Cost (extra money is needed to fix the delay).

Response plan:

-Mitigation: Choose good and reliable contractors and make a clear contract.

-Contingency: Have a backup plan to hire another team or work overtime.

2.Staff turnover

-Description: New staff quit their jobs soon after training.

-Possible impacts: Quality (service is bad), Cost (more money to hire new people).

Response plan:

-Mitigation: Give good pay and make a nice workplace.

-Contingency: Keep a backup list of workers and train them quickly.

3.Low sales revenue

-Description: Customers do not visit the stores, so the stores do not break even in 8 months.

-Possible impacts: Cost (less money is made, project loses money).

Response plan:

-Mitigation: Do research before choosing store locations.

-Contingency: Run sales and discounts to get more customers.
