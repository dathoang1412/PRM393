Request 1:
1. Project name: Coi Cafe expansion in 5 Central provinces
2. Justifications:
- Coi's management decided to implement project in 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) to survey, select premises, design and construct interiors to development and expansion the Coi Cafe brand in other provinces.
3. Project constraints:
- In terms of scope: The project scope includes: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store.
- Time: The project duration is strictly limeted in 12 months.
- Cost: The project total cost is capped at VND 8.5 billion with VND 1.7 billion VND for each store from equity and commercial bank loan.
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening. 

Request 2:
- Five main cost/budget items:

1. The cost to build flagship store 
- The cost of maintain store, electric , water in 12 months
- Esimated: Approximately 500.000.000 VND billion 
- Way to estimate: Based on position of store and provinces 
- Person in charged: Market Development Director

2. The cost to hire and train staff
- The budget to hire staff, recruiting and training local personel.
- Estimated: Approximately 200.000.000 VND billion
- Way to estimate: Based on program training and the amount of staff each store
- Person in charged: HR Leader

3. The cost to design and construct the brand
- The cost to design and contruct interiors consistent with the brand
- Estimated: Approximately 300.000.000 VND billion
- Way to estimate: Based on what design leader want to implement
- Person in charged: Design Leader

4. The cost to survey and select permises
- The cost to conduct survey and select data
- Estimated: Approximately 100.000.000 VND billion 
- Way to estimate: Hire people to conduct survey, and create a program to select data
- Person in charged: Marketing Leader

5. The cost to building raw material supply chain and lauching campaigns
- The cost to build a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
- Estimated: Approximately 400.000.000 VND billion
- Way to estimate: Connect with central region suppliers and launching campaigns
- Person in charged: Market Development Director

- Description: The duration of project will be stretch.
- Impact: The time of project will be affected by deadlines of each store,  and the cost maybe affected.
- Mitigation: Should put deadlines for each scope to each store can be on time with deadlines.

- Description: Teamwork not good between design team, HR team, marketing team.
