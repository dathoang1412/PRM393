Request 1: Project charter statement
Project name: Coi Cafe project.

Detailed description: a pure Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, famous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model (using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers). Coi's management decided to implement an  expansion project into 5 Central provinces, with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.

Purpose: to sell traditional drink menu, give customer  an experience of nostalgic space.

Constraint:
 - Scope: 
         +Implementing an expansion project into 5 Central provinces, with 1 flagship store.
         +Constructing interiors consistent with the brand.
        +Recruiting and training local personnel.
        +Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store.
 -Time: within 12 months.
 - Cost/ budget: The total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion / store).
 - Quality: 
         + Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
         + Using reusable materials.
         + Well-organized training local personnel.
         + Aesthetic interior design matching with the brand.
Request 2: Main cost/ Budget items:
Budget item 1: Investing natural and qualified coffee bean.
- Description: Qualified coffee enhances beverage menu's quality and gain customer's satisfaction.
- Estimation: 2 billion VND 
- Person in charged: Market Development Director.

Budget item 2: Resuable materials for cups
- Description: Materials that can be used for recycled for friendly investment.
- Estimation: 1.5 billion VND.
- Person in charged: Market Development Director.

Budget item 3: Sustainable materials for interiors.
- Description: Able to maintained interior quality for a long term.
- Estimation: 3 billion VND.
- Person in charged: Project manager

Budget item 4: Business rental money for each store.
-Description: Paying rental business to run the stores legally.
-Estimation: 1 billion VND.
- Person in charged: Project manager.

Budget item 5: Money to rent sellers and staffs
-Description: Rent sellers and staffs with monthly salary to maintain the brand business.
-Estimation: 1 billion VND
- Person in charged: Project manager.

Request 3: Project risks

Risk 1:  Interior design may not be consistent with brand (scope)
- Description: Some province required adequate interior design for each place to fit for it's local and this may conflict with the brand design 
- Probability: High.
- Impact: Medium.
- Mitigation: Make sure the design still holds the authenticity for the brand 
- Contigency: Try to combine both which suitable for design local province and original brand design to enhance interior creativity.

Risk 2: Reusable cup materials may be damaged (cost)
- Description: Cups reuse for a long time may be damaged due to fragility
- Probabilty: High 
- Impact: High 
- Mitigation: Have to invest more resuable cups
- Contigency: Switch different materials which can be recycled and usable again with less costly requirement.

Risk 3: Delayed constructing and building for store expansion.(time)
- Description: Contructing may be delayed due to insufficient building materials. 
- Probability: High. 
- Impact: High. 
- Mitigation: Switch to rental available house to shorten the constructing progress.
- Contigency: Focusing on interior material preparation if switching to rental housing
