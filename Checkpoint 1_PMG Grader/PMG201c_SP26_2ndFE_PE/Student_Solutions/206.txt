1. Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion
2.
- Purpose: Implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) with one flagship store in each province and launch campaigns for each store.

3. Constrains:
+ Scope: Open new cofffee store in 5 Central provinces
+Time: Within 12 months,
+ Cost/Budget: VND 8.5 billion from equity and commercial bank loans 
+ Quality: Reach break-even point within 8 months after opening.
 
Request 2: 
6 main cost budget items:
1. Infrastructure cost
+ Description: Monthly amount of money to sell coffee
+ Estimation: Analogous (VND 200 million/store)
+ Person in charge: Branch management

2. Designing and interiors cost
+ Description: Designing and buying appropriate store's item to fit the nostalgic vibe and each province's culture.
+ Estimation: Bottom-up (about VND 500 million/store)
+ Person in charge: Design Leader

3. Hiring and training cost
+ Description: Budget to recruit and training local personnel for the local store.
+ Estimation: Parametric, Cost = Training + ( Salary * Amount of person)
+ Person in charge: Store manager

4. Marketing and advertising cost
+ Description: Promote the shop in order to attract customer in each province
+ Estimation: Analogous, based on previous marketing campaigns (about VND 50 million/store)
+ Person in charge: Marketing Manager

5. Create and adjust menu cost
+ Description: Analyze the local's taste and create new products or modify existing products.
+ Estimaion: Analogous, based on previous attempts.
+ Person in charge: Store manager 

6. Store Construction cost
+ Description: main budget to build up the coffee store
+ Estimation: Analogous based on previous buildings with 20% more cost (roughly VND 1 billion/store)
+ Person in charge: Construction leader

Request 3:
1. Infrastructure price risk (cost)
- Description: The rapid increase of infrastructure cost in good places may increase any time.
- Impact:
+ Infrastructure cost exceeds the budget
- Mitigation: Increase the budget for infrastructure, pick the destination before the project's initiation.
-  Contigency: Reduce menu cost and marketing cost or pick a backup place.

2. Chaotic during grand opening (quality)
- Description: Due to inexperience local staff and the outbreak of grand opening ceremory, products and services may not be able to meet up to customer's expectancy.
- Impact:
+ Bad experience for customer
+ Bad review in social media
- Mitigaton: Hire experienced manager, create backup plan.
- Contigency: Hire part-time employee, limit the ammout of customer and post notification in social media to make amend for the customers.

3. Construction delay risk (time) 
- Description: Unexpected weather, storm, pandemic disrupts the construction process.
- Impact:
+ Delay planned opening time
+ Increase of labour cost
+ Delay the other steps
- Mitigation: Select appropriate season in every province to begin building, spend more budget in construction to complete fast.
- Contigency: Delay opening, completing the crucial parts first, then complete the reamaining later.
