Request 1: Project narrative charter
-Project Name: Coi Cafe Central

-Justification:
+Detail description:
Coi Cafe,a pure Vietnamese coffee brand in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City,famous and sustainable bussiness model decides to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces,with 1 flagship store in each province within a year.
+Reason: 
The company want to expand the coffee brand into other province in order to make the brand more popular among people.

-Project constraint:
+Scope:
Open 1 flagship store in each provinces.
Survey and select premises.
Design and construct interiors consistent with the brand.
Recruit and train local personnel.
Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
Launching campaigns for each store.
+Time:12 months
+Cost/Budget:VND 8.5 billion(approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans
+Quality:Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.


Request 2:Five main cost/budget items

-Item 1:
+Name:Coffee store
+Description:Finding the place that the Coi Cafe company rent to build the brand and selling coffee
+Estimation:100 VND million/store. 
+How to estimate:Finding place that in central of the provinces,that can be easily find out by young and adult. Must be addressed on the main or busy street for easy access.
+Person in charged: Project manager,marketing team.

-Item 2:
+Name:Coffee
+Description:The main coffee type for making coffee
+Estimation:70 VND million/store. The company using the same coffee brand as Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City store,but have to survey Central customer to know their taste and flavour in order to change the coffee material.
+How to estimate: The company can create more coffee flavour and type to increase the survey product to know the local tast, then decide which type of coffee will be used.
+Person in charged:Project manager.

-Item 3:
+Name:Marketing the brand
+Desciption:Advertise the Coi Cafe to people in Central Provinces
+Estimation:200-300 VND million/store.The company have to make commercial advertisement to make the brand more popular. 
+How to estimate:The marketing team have to contact with local marketing store to make billboard amd image. And finding place to rent for adding those billboards,images(mostly in the center of the city,or on the main street for people to see)
+Person in charged:Marketing team

-Item 4:
+Name:Interior product,furniture
+Description:Buying new tables,chairs, kitchen products(coffee machine, POST system machine,..) to run the bussinesss 
+Estimation:300-400 VND million/store. The company have to buy brand new products and machines.
+How the estimate:Contact with funitures, machines company to buy products, and calculate all the fee(products price, transfer prices) 
+Person in charged:Project manager, interior design team

-Item 5:
+Name: Register POST system
+Desciption:The store have to find and register a POST system(like Alobo,KioViet,Sabo)
+Estimation:10 VND million/store. 
+How to estimate:Contact with POST website to know about there policy and system work. Decide which is suitable for the store.
+Person in charged:Project manager

Request 3: Project risks

-Risk 1:
+Title/Name:People do not care about the coffee brand
+Description:Viet Nam is a coffee place where many famous and local coffee brand appear. People in Central region can not pay enough attension  for a new coffee brand
+Possible impact:High .Reason is that if this occur,the brand can suffer a big dropdown in income and have to shutdown early.
+Mitigation:Increase in marketing, make people to see the brand more often, create more advertisement on social media(like Facebook,Youtube,...)
+Contigency:Survey and try to know why people do not pay attension to the brand(maybe because they do not know, or they just stick to local coffee brand)  in order to re-plan how the brand run.

-Risk 2:
+Title/Name:The price of the product is too overprice compare with local
+Description:The Coi Cafe is a supiror coffee chain with nostalgic space(decorated space for youth to hang out and take picture) so the price of the product will include that category.That mean the price will higher than normal local coffee brand. The product also use good,high quality material to make, which also be included into the price, pushing it even higher.
+Possible impact:High. Reason is that most people just want to have coffee with competitive price(low price with medium/high quality coffee).
+Mitigation:Decrease the quality in service as low as possible, which mean that the store have to lower the price or the product quality/quantity,or marketing that the product is made of good,high quality material so that the customer can understand what standard the store is.
+Contingency:Create more product on menu with varied prices with different material quality for different type of customer.

-Risk 3:
+Title/Name:Losing rental space
+Description:The owner of the place(where the store is built and rent) can take the place back to sell or re-rent it to orther bussiness company that pay higher money
+Possible impact:High. Reason is that the brand will lost the store and have to find other place, which will cost more time and money. The brand will lost customer intersts because of moving to other place.
+Mitigation:Talk calmly with the rental onwer to make deal again, tell the local goverment to step in if the rental owner have bad behavior in the contract, read the contract slowly and sign after chekcing all the related information.
+Contingency:The manager of each store have to find other rental space that suitable and availble to move if needed, remain contact with other rental owner.
