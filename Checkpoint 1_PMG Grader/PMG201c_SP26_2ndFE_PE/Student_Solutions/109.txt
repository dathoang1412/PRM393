1. Project name:
Coi Cafe - Bring pure Vietnamese coffee around VietNam

Justifications:
In the context Coi Cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee  and famous for its nostalgic space , traditional drink menu , and sustaibale business model . This project aims to bring a pure Vietnameese coffee into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue , Quang Nam , Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) 

The purpose of this project is survey and select premises , design and constructing interiors consistent with the brand and recruiting and trainning local personel , building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and lauching campaigns for each store. 

Project constraints:
Scope:
 - survey and selecting premises 
 - designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
 - recruiting and training local personnel
 - building a raw material supply chain form Central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store 
 
Time: 
- The project will lauch 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months

Budget:
- The project total budget is VND 8.5 billion 

Quality:
- Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening
