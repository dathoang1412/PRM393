Request 3: Three project risks:
    1) Budget overrun
       -Description: when the budget is higher than the total budget
       -Probabilities: Medium
       -Impact: the cost is higher
       -Mitigation: make a note to write what they have spend money for, prepare a plan for buying,
       -Contigency: Use extra money, decrease the budget of other activities

    2) Low rate of customers
       -Description: when the rate of customers is not as high as the brand's expectation
       -Probabilities: Medium
       -Impact: the income can be not as expected
       -Mitigation: Hire marketing team, make some hot deals for the store, and give the beneficials to gold customers. make events
       -Contigency: Focus on improve the quaility of the store, make big sales to attract customers,...

     3) Employees withdrawal
      -Description: when suddenly a lot of the employees leave the work
      -Probabilities: Low
      -Impact: the quality goes down because there aren't many employees left
                    the time and cost increase because the brand has to hire and training employees again
      -Mitigate: Give employees good beneficials, recruit extra employees,...
      -Contigency: re-allocate other employees from other stores to the store that has a few employees, recruit new employees,...
