Request 3:
Risk 1: Fully depend on Dak Lak fammers.
- Regarding traditional drink menu and sustainable business model that are Coi Cafe USP. So if have any problem or even is drama from supplier, this can lead to reduce the number of our customers and branding suffer a significant pain.
- Solution: Find more suppliers, not just depend on one or two of them. The rationale is when have any issue we can cancel the contract with least damages for the company. The money to build the brand is one of the most vital to survive.
Risk 2: The attitude of Gen Z staff in work.
- In this generation, most of youth people not care too much about their job and specially is part-time job. This can lead the result in their behavior and attitude often can not control easily when face in difficult case. Then the store can be rate 1 star or have a bigger problem with customer and branding.
- Solution: We should separate to have a low leader for a small group of staffs and just let that leader to take responsibilities when have issues. Additionally, the HR team have to be divided and work all time to solve the probem around employees as soon as possible.
Risk 3: The raw material supply chain
- How it terrible when a customer come in a cafe and can not order a expresso just because run out of coffee bean. 
- Solution: Building and having a detail of stratergy in supply chain form central region supplier to each flagship store. We can use 3PL or 4PL in this stratergy to ensure the delay as less as possible.
