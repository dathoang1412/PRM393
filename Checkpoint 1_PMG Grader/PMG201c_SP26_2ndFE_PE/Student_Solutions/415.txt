Request 1:
Project name: Coi Cafe Central Expansion Project 2026.
This project is to open 5 new stores in Central Vietnam. One store in each place: Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh. The main purpose is to grow the brand and reach more customers. Coi Cafe already strong in Hanoi and HCM, so now expand is needed. Also, Central area is growing fast, many young people and tourist. This can bring more profit and brand awarness. Another reason is to build local supply chain, buy coffee from nearby farmers, reduce cost a bit and keep sustainable ideas.
Project has some constraints. First is scope. Only 5 stores, not more. Each must follow same concept: nostalgic design, traditional menu, eco style. Second is time. Total project must finish in 12 months, so quite tight. Third is cost. Budget is 8.5 billion VND, approximately VND 1.7 billion/store, cannot overspend. Last is quality. Store must look same brand image, drinks must keep same taste, service need training well. If quality drop, brand image will be bad.

Request 3:
Risk 1: Delay in construction
Description: contractor finish late or problem with material.
Impact: time delay, cost increase (pay more rent, labor), scope maybe reduce decor.
Response:
Mitigation: choose trusted contractor, clear contract, weekly check progress. 
Contingency: prepare backup team or adjust opening date. 
Risk 2: Cannot reach expected revenue
Description: new market maybe low demand or wrong location.
Impact: affect quality (cut cost), time to break-even longer, cost pressure high.
Response:
Mitigation: do good market research, choose good location, strong marketing. 
Contingency: run promotion, adjust menu price, reduce some cost. 
Risk 3: Supply chain problem
Description: lack of materials or delay delivery from supplier.
Impact: affect quality (no same taste), time delay in opening, extra cost.
Response:
Mitigation: have 2–3 suppliers, sign contract early. 
Contingency: use backup supplier, keep safety stock.
