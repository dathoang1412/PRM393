Request 1:
- Project Name: Coi Coffee Store Campaign.
- Justification: In the morden social, everyone have a lot of choice to entertainment such as: watching films, enjoying popular foods,...Besides, Coffee is one of the most popular place to relax together. Therefore, Coi Cafe is a pure coffee brand in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, famous for its nostagic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model.
- Purpose: Coi Coffee decided to implement an expansion project into five Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with one flagship store in each province within 12 months. This is a perfectly place to many people especially teenagers go to coffee store to experience and enjoy many traditional drink and food menu. Morever. teenagers can go to the coffee store to study and unwind because this place can become workplace.
- Scope: This project includes: 
Surveying and selecting premises.
Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand.
Recruiting and training local personnel.
Building a raw material supply chain form Central region suppliers.
Launching campaigns for each store.
- Time: 12 months (1 flagship store in each province within 12 months).
- Cost/Budget: The total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billiion/store).
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
