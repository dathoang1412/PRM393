Request 1: Narrative charter
Project name: Coi Cafe expansion project
Justificattions:
- Purpose: To implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces of Coi Cafe(Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.
- Reasons: Coi's management want to expand 5 Central of Coi Cafe
Project Constraints:
- Scope: 
 + Surveying and selecting premises
 + Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
 + Recruiting and training local personel
 + Building a raw meterial supply chain from Central region suppliers
 + Launching campaigns for each store
- Time:  12 months
- Cost/budget: 8.5 billion VND( 1.7 billion/store)
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening

Request 2: Main cost/budget items
Cost/budget item 1:
- Name: Cost for maketing and promotion
- Description: Spreading the event
- Estimation method: Research about maketing and promotion cost
- Estimated cost: VND 20 millions
- Person in charged: Lead of maketing team

Cost/budget item 2:
- Name: Employees and training fees
- Description: Cost to pay the empoyees
- Estimation method: Hourly rate x total hours x total days
- Estimated cost: VND 50 millions
- Person in charged: Lead of personnel management

Cost/budget item 3:
- Name: Design and constructing interiors cost
- Description: Cost to pay the material needed to design and constructing interiors
- Estimation method: Total all cost to pay material
- Estimated cost: VND 300 millions
- Person in charged: Lead of designing team

Cost/budget item 4: 
- Name: Prepare raw coffee from Daklak 
- Description: Purchase coffee prepare for opening event
- Estimation method: Directly from seller
- Estimated cost: VND 100 millions
- Person in charged: PM or lead of market team

Cost/budget item 5:
- Name: Inventory cost
- Description: Cost to rent a inventory
- Estimation method: Research from maket place
- Estimated cost: VND 50 millions
- Person in charged: Lead of market team

Request 3: Project Risks
Project risk 1:
- Name: Over budget
- Description: Out of budget before ending the project
- Possibility: MEDIUM
- Impact: HIGH
- Possible impact: Can not reach break-even point within 8 months
- Mitigation: Carefully analyze budget, and the cost from begining
- Contingency: Use reserve cost, lay off some employee, reduce unneccessaries event

Project risk 2:
- Name: Satisfication of customers is low
- Description: Customers not satisfy with the store or the designing of the store
- Possibility: HIGH
- Impact: HIGH
- Possible impact: impact the revenue of the store
- Mitigation: Researching from market trend, surveying customers about the idea before start project
- Contigency: Add more drink to menu, create more event

Project risk 3:
- Name: Do not have enough personel
- Description: Lack of employees working in store
- Possibility: MEDIUM
- Impact: MEDIUM
- Possible impact: Reduce quality of secducing customers
- Mitigation: Maketing with high salary
- Contigency: Tranfer employees from another store
