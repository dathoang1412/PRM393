Request 1 
Project Name:
 Central Region Store Development Project – Coi Caf
Purpose / Justification:
 The project is designed to establish five new stores in Central Vietnam over a 12-month period. These stores will be located in major provinces to capture both local and tourist markets.
This initiative aims to grow the company’s presence nationwide, improve revenue performance, and build a localized supply network to support sustainable operations.
Success Criteria:
Complete 5 stores within 12 months
Achieve break-even within 8 months
Ensure consistent customer experience
Stay within allocated budget
Stakeholders:
 Management team, Project Manager, HR, Marketing, Design, Suppliers
Constraints:
 Scope limited to 5 stores, time limited to 1 year, budget fixed, quality must follow brand
