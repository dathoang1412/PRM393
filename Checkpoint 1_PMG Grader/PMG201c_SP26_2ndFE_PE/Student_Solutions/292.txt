Request 3: Risk Management

1. Risk about budget
- Risk title: Overate budget
- Impact: Budgets for decoration, resources, marketing, ads, training each store could be overrated.
- Probability: 70%
- Migrate Action: Have plaining costs clearly, PM need to review all costs for each items before approve.
- Contingency Action: reduce costs marketing and ads, reduce numbers of employess, reduce quantity of decorations.

2. Risk about competitive
- Risk title: Competitve others brands
- Impact: Competitve others big brands like starbucks, highlands,.. and may be some street-cafe.
- Probability: 50%
- Migrate Action: Survey and selecting premises clearly, reduce place a lot of cafe in around.
- Contingency Action: Improve marketing campaigms, build virals shorts video on social media, apply more promotions.

3. Risk about storms
- Risk title: Storms
- Impact: With 5 Centrals Province (Da Nang, Huy, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) often have storms.
- Probability: 70%
- Migrate Action: Research and building clearly constructing flagship store can resists if face storm in the future.
- Contingency Action: Help local personal to resists storm, have "Quy Tu Thien" for people, and have some promotions for customer.
