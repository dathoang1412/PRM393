R1: 
1. 

Project name: Coi Cafe's implement and expansion project ( pure Vietnamese coffee)

2. Justification: 

Reasons: increasing awareness and encouraging a famous coffee in Dak Lak ( a pure Vietnamese coffee) with a unique taste, as well as introducing this product with overseas customers 

Purposes: 
Approaching in 5 central provinces ( Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) through touch points ( flagship, stores, campaign in each stores)

3. Project constraints

In term of scope: 
- Surveying and selecting premises 
- Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand 
- Recruiting and training local personnel 
- Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers 
- Launching campaign for 5 stores 

Duration 
Constructing a flagship store for each Central provinces (total of 5) within 12 months. In addition, each store must reach the break-even point in 8 months after opening 

Cost/Budget:
- The total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/ store) from equity and commercial bank loans

Quality: 
- Having 90% of customer's satisfaction via their feedbacks after operating stores  
- Running this project on schedule and do not overstate with initial budget  
- Providing perfectly coffee products for 5 stores with little or not risks of supply chains 

R2: 

5 main cost/budget items 

Total budget: VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/ store)

1. Renting venue 
- Description: a offline place to reach local customers with trading activities and experiencing Coi Cafe's products  
- Estimation: approximately VND 340 million (depend on different Central provinces)
- Estimating method: analogous estimating - base on venue surveys are conducted in each Central province 
- In charge: Project management 

2. Labor cost
- Description: individuals have responsible for serving customers, cleaning space, introducing products with customers,... at stores 
- Estimation: approximately VND 340 millions/month ( 4 per/store)     
- Estimating method: parametric method - base on renting staff cost in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh city  
- In charge: Project management 

3. Printing material (in marketing)
Description: designing and printing marketing materials such as flyers, catalogue, posters,... serve in launching campaign
Estimation: approximately 340 million 
Estimating method: parametric method - base on relevant project was conducted before 
In charge: marketing team

4. Building a raw material supplu chain
- Description: a region place to storage and supply raw material for 5 flagship stores that save effort if Coi Cafe take coffee from Dak Lak 
- Estimation: approximately VND 340 million
- Estimating method: analogous method
- In charge: project management 



5. Contingency reserve
- Description: contingency fund to resolve emergency risks which can not predicted
- Estimation: approximately 340 million 
- Estimating method: contingency reserve 
- In charge: project management 

R3: 

Overdue scheduled
- Desciption: progress project is not on time schedule  because occuring interuptions such as suppliers delivery lately products,...
- Possible impacts: have not products to buy for customers, maketing activities delays to make products reach customer non-effectively 
- Mitigation plan: monitoring tasks and manage time tightly between progress od departments
- Contingency plan: add more members or increase time-working 

Overstated budget
- Desription: do not ensure budget in VND 8.5 billion compare to prior plan 
- Possible impacts: can not delay tasks if project management still have not solution immediately or seriously this project can not continue 
- Mitigation plan: control tightly all costs, report monthly to project management and avoid to small errors in transaction 
- Contingency plan: always have 10% contingency budget in total


Conflict relationship in interior teams 
- Desciption: communication in between departments non-effectively, working independently 
- Possible impacts: working system of project are interupted because department can not understand current tasks which they have to coordinate each other
- Mitigation plan: provide basic rules in working space such as be always respect and listen different opinions
- Contingency plan: add more new members if having several members want to quit their task
