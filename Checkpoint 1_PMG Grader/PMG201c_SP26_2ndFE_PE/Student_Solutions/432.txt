1. The project name is Coi Cafe's emotion and enjoying

2. Due to the current fact of Coi Cafe, its potential to widely open in many provinces of VietNam, not just stop with stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City, because of its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model by using reusable cups, purchasing coffe and the materials directly from Dak Lak farmers; for many people can go through and drink, as well as chill with the signature space of this brand. 

From all of that, we decide to do this project to enhance this brand - Coi Cafe, with some purposes as raise everybody's awareness about the history, a pure Vietnamese coffee brand, the services of drink this brand has, and in the future, people will choose Coi Cafe in order to enjoy the moment and relax time; increase the revenues and profits and the goods/drinks that come to customers.

3. About project constraints
- Terms of scope and time: This project  focuses on 5 central provices as Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh within 12 months.  

- The total budget for this project is 8.5 billion VND, approximately 1.7 billion VND for one store from equity and commercial bank loans.

- The quality of the project includes surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain form Central region suppliers, and launching campagins for each store.

Request 3:

Some project risks of this project are:
1. Person suddenly quit off the work 
- This can be an accident at any provinces in the project's scope

- This will affects many things as the timeline the project can be done, if anything happens, the project might last longer, more than 12 months. If the PM is out, there is a big risk, people donnot know what to do next, or is this design good and suitable or not. And can effect about the famous, the reputation with people of the team.

- To mitigate this, the PM has to work and communicate with all people in the project, listen and pay attention to find out the solution if necessary. Along with that, PM always focus on the tasks and the people along, to find out the problems (if have), support them if they need.

2. Overated budget 
- This can be strongly affect for  the whole project. Due to the scope of the project quite strong and widely (through 5 provinces). If there is any issue about the budget, the whole project can be effected, both times and money. This will not make the project be done on time.

- The plans to response to this risk can be: Always reporting to the management regularly, maybe weekly, or each one day in a week, or monthly reports about all the cost that is used. And always checking that is there anything not in the right way, or is every items of the budget like we make the plan.

3. Quality of the product (can be the designs, or the goods, the drinks)
- Because our material is nature from farmers, while the weather cannot be like the forcasts team said before. And if there is any accident that effect to the quality of the material, the taste of the drinks we bring to customer can be different. This will effect a lots to the quality of the brands, customer might not come with us anymore.

- The risk response plan for this risk might be: grow indoors with the fixed test. And always checking the plant, the material with its quality. If it is anything not good when we check the quality, we can throw those are not quality enough, only use those are quality.
