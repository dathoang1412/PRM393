Request 1:
Project name: Coi Cafe expansion for Central provinces and flagship store.
The purpose of this project is to implement an expansion into 5 Central provinces with 1 flagship store in each one withing 12 months.
This project will help enhance the brand appearance with unique business model, interesting customer in using resuable cups, nostalgic space and create a change for expand customer variance.
The following constraints of this project include:
- Scope constraint is surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personal, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and lanching campaigns for each store.
- Time constraint of the project is within 12 months.
- Cost/Budget constraint is VND 8.5 billion from equity and commercial bank loans.
- Quality constraint is to reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

The following 5 main cost/budget items:
1. Surveying and selection premises:
- Create a survey for customer selection, attractive point in the product, willness for paying.

2. Designing and constructing interior:

5. Lauching attractive campaigns for new appearance for each store.

Request 3:
The following risk that may affect the project:
1. The number of customers is larger than expected so the store have no enought space for them.
- This is the positive threat, which is the chance to build-up the store but need to pay more for expand the store, recruit and train the personel, etc.
2. The signature product do not atract local customer so that the earned values may be not enought to keep the store working.
3. The store do not reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

The project roles:
