Request 1: 
-Project name:  Expanding the brand throughout Vietnam. 

-Justification: This project is conducted to gain more market share in the F&B industry, make more money, and enhance the brand's reputation. The central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh DInh) have large populations, which is suitable for reaching a large number of customers and generates more money. 

-Project constraints: 
*Scope:1 flagship store in each province (5 provinces in total) 
*Time: All stores must be built within 12 months 
*Cost/Budget: Total budget is VND 8.5 billion ( approximately VND 1.7 Billion/store)
*Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Request 3: Three project risks: 
- Exceeding the budget: 
*Description: the costs for establishing a flagship store exceeds 1.7 billion. 

* Its impact: It takes more time to work with the accounting team to adjust the budget. Sometimes, there is not enough money available for the project to continues. 

*Plans: Should prepare more money to cover unexpected costs. The total budget should be  1.7 billion VND/store + 50 million VND ( to cover unexpected costs) 


- The market development director suddenly quits the job ( because he/she is under a lot of pressure and can't handle it, he/she has diseases and needs to be treated,... )

* Its impact: The activities can not be coordinated, and no one can oversee the entire project. The interior design team, HR team, and Marketing team might lose sight of a bigger picture. 

-Plan: Offer the Market Development Director with higher and better perks, benefits. Choose a suitable person to replace the director if he/ she can't continue to the work because of diseases, accidents,...  


- Wrong message to customers: 
* Description: The brand delivers the wrong message to customers. For example, In a donation, they say they would give 1000 VND for each cup they sells. Meanwhile, they sell the cup for 50,000 VND, which is much more than the money they agree to donate to chairy

*Its impact: Damaging the brand reputation. This will lead to a decrease in revenue. 

* Plan: Before the marketing team runs the advertisement, they must carefully chose the words and message to delivers. Besides, messages delivered to customers must be honest and clear.
