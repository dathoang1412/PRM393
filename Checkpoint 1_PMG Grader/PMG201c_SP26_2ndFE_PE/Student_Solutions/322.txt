Request 1:
     1. Project name: Coi Cafe
     2. Justification:
          Purpose: to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.
     3. Projectconstraints:
          - Scope:
               + Surveying and selecting premises.
               + Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand.
               + Recruiting and training local personnel.
               + Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
               + Launching campaigns for each store.
          - Time: within 12 months
          - Budget: VND 8.5 billion
          - Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.


Request 2:
     Five main cost/budget items:
          1. Cost/budget item 1:
               + Name: Survey and select premises.
               + Description: choose the good place with the potential customers around to get profits.
               + Estimation: target some places that might place the store, then follow up  locals living nearby about their habit of getting coffee, and which customers might be our potential customers in the future. Responsibility: Market Development Director.
          2. Cost/budget item 2:
               + Name: Design and construct interiors consistent with the brand.
               + Description:design and setup store belongs to the ideas of the brand.
               + Estimation: meeting with the brand to start design and construct interiors consistent; hire a design and construction team to build up stores in each provice. Responsibility: the interior design team.
          3. Cost/budget item 3:
               + Name: Hiring staffs (barista, waiter, store manager,...).
               + Description: recruite and training local personnel based on the requirements od the brand.
               + Estimation: post hiring news into finding jobs websites (TopCV, careerViet), social media (Threads, Facebook); organize some training classes before opening. Responsibility: HR team.
          4. Cost/budget item 4:
               + Name: A raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
               + Desscription; building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
               + Estimation: finding suppliers that have good quality materials, reasonable prices and use the same material brands as Coi Cafe does with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City to get the same quality. Responsibility: Market Development Director.
          5. Cost/budget item 5:
               + Name: launching campaigns for each store.
               + Description: create creative ideas suitable to each provice, making videos to launch campaigns for each store.
                + Estimation: plan contents, make videos, post campaign into social media, choose contents that bring somthing related to each province to make more creative. Responsibility: Marketing team.

Request 3:
     1. Risk 1:
          - Name: Lack of staffs
          - Description: hiring is not enough for each store, lack of staffs, or they do not fluent enough when in a busy hour.
          - Possible impacts:
               + Scope/quality: recruiting and training local personnel
               + Time: every months if they come and go
               + Cost:VND 70 million
     2. Risk 2:
          - Name: Backup plan for a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
          - Description: Finding more than 1 supplier for each material just in case.
          - Possible impacts:
               + Scope/quality: affect drink menu and store.
               + Time: from July to end of September
               + Cost: VND 200 million
     3. Risk 3:
          - Name: Planning for rain season and storm
          - Description: Central provices are always in a part of the affect of rain season and storm from July to September.
          - Possible impacts:
               + Scope/quality: maybe close in a while and have to repair the stores.
               + Time: from July to end of September
               + Cost: VND 1 billion
