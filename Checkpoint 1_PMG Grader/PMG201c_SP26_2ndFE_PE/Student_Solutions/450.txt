request 1
project name: coi cafe expansion project

justifications:
coi cafe has pride themselves for many years on their traditional drink menu, nostalgic spaces and uses practices to make the world greener such as using reusable cups and using organic coffee beans that comes directly on the farm.

the project aims to expand coi cafe's influence across other central provinces besides hanoi and ho chi minh city, further strengthening the brand's visibility in the mind of the local community with their business models.

project constraints:

scope: the activities within the project include surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, bulding a raw material supply chain from central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store.

time: the duration expansion must stay within 12 months, with each store reaching break-even point within 8 months after opening

cost/budget: total budget for the project is 8.5 billion vnd with approximately 1.7 biilion per store, which already includes fees for supplies, training staffs and resources needed for the campaigns.

quality: the quality of the products must not deterred from what have already been established (organic coffee beans, using reusables items)

request 2

training local personnel
description: fees for recruiting and training the staff in each province in preparation for the opening of each store
budget: 12 million vnd
estimation: analogous estimating. using the baseline fee use for employess. (in charge: hr team)

launching campaigns' materials
description: fees needed for the campaigns such as flyers, stand-in welcome mascot (if needed), banners,...
budget: 8 million vnd
estimation: vendor bid analysis. (in charge: marketing team)

interior design materials
description: fees for hiring and working with an interior design team along with the materials needed for constructing the stores
budget: 20 million vnd
estimation: bottom-up estimating. (in charge: interior design team)

quality supplies from the supply chain
description: effectively communicating with suppliers, making sure the materials from the supply chain buildings has satisfactory qualities
budget: 30 million vnd
estimation: parametric estimating (in charge: project manager)

request 3

wrong estimations for the budget or cost overruns
probability: low
impact: highly affects the scope and time of the project
mitigation plan: adding a buffer of 5% to the budget, try and communicate with the higher ups to recalculate the budget and make changes.
contingency plan: add a contingency reserve fund from the beginning.

project went behind the schedule
probability: medium
impact: medium
mitigation plan: add more staffs to do different tasks at the same time, this method will add more to the personnel budget, contacting higher ups to add more time for the project
contingency plan: plan the tasks effectively, continuously monitoring for any mistake to avoid delaying the schedule.

project manager has poor management skills
probability: low
impact: high
mitigation plan: immediately train and update the project manager with the necessary skills needed, this process may affects the time constraint.
contingency plan: replace with an entirely new project manager
