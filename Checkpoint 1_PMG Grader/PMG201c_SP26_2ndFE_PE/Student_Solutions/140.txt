Request 1

Project name: Coi Cafe Central Vietnam Expansion Project

Coi Cafe plans to expand to 5 Central provinces: Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, and Binh Dinh. The project will open 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months. The project is managed by the Market Development Director as Project Manager. The PM works with design team, HR team, marketing team, and suppliers.

The purpose of this project is to grow the business and reach more customers in Central Vietnam. It also helps increase brand awareness and sales. Central Vietnam has many customers such as tourists, students, and workers.

Project scope includes location survey, selecting premises, designing and building stores, hiring and training staff, building supply chain, and running marketing campaigns.

Time constraint: 12 months to open all 5 stores.
Cost constraint: total budget is VND 8.5 billion.
Scope constraint: must open 5 stores in 5 provinces.
Quality constraint: stores must follow brand style and reach break-even within 8 months.

Request 3

Risk 1: Cannot find good location
Description: hard to find suitable premises
Impact:
scope or quality: location is not good
time: delay opening
cost: higher rent
Mitigation: survey early and prepare backup locations
Contingency: choose second best location

Risk 2: Construction delay
Description: building work is late
Impact:
scope or quality: design may be reduced
time: delay opening
cost: extra cost for labor
Mitigation: choose good contractor and check progress
Contingency: add more workers or change contractor

Risk 3: Staff not ready
Description: cannot hire enough staff
Impact:
scope or quality: service is not good
time: delay opening
cost: more training cost
Mitigation: recruit early and use training plan
Contingency: use experienced staff from other cities
