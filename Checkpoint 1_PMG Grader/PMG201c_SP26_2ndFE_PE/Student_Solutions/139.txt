Request 1:
- Project name: Morning coffee with a care for our planet
- Justification: This project's purpose is to spread the brand to whole country, bringing a new atomosphere while drinking coffee with resuable cup and knowledge about protect enviroment. Building a raw material supply chain from 5 central regions (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) and a flagship store in each of them also recruiting and training local personnel.
- Contrains: 
   + In term of scope we will spread our Coi Cafe brand for more and let people know how to protect our planet. We won't build a whole factory in each region to get ingredients in other hand we will buy from local farmers with a more comfortable price.  
   + This project will be held for 12 months.
   + The total budget is 8.5 billion VND (approximately 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.
   + Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Request 3:
Risk 1:
Name: Late for deadlines (could not find platform for rent or late when finding employees).
Impact: Time and cost
Mitigation: Focus on others region.
Contingency: Form 5 teams for each region each have 3 - 5 members. When some of the region finish they tasks (for example we just only  found a platform for rent in Da Nang, Hue but others not) let 1 - 2 member to other place that haven't finished.

Risk 2:
Name: Lack of teamwork in project team.
Impact: Quality
Mitigation: A leader which is PM must check all the task that he/she have seperated for memebers and quickly take in charge for it.
Contingency: PM must have conversation with each member in the team after that conduct a meeting to solve the problem within members (for example member being missunderstanding with each other).

Risk 3:
Name: Uncondition weather give bad quality of ingredients or late supply.
Impact: Quality and cost.
Mitigation: Closed the store for short days if we have no more ingredients.
Contingency: Each store must have own stored supply for emergency and the ingredients must meet the good quality when we serve to customer.
