Project name: Coi Cafe into Central provinces.
1.2 
The purpose of this project is to implement an expansion project into 5 Central province within 12 months, with budget about VND 8.5 billion, This project aim to surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors cosistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
1.3 
-Scope constraints: Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project int 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).

-Time constraints: this project implement and completed in exactly 12 months (1 flagship store in each province).

-Cost constrants: the total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans. And ensure the cost not exceed.

-Quality constrants: due to customers saticfation when they uses product from store, and had a least 80% an active feedback on survey, increase awerness for Coi cafe brand on customers, surveying and selecting premises, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
