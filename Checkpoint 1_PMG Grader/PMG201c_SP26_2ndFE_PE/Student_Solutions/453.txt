Request 2: Definition of Budget items for CoiCafe Expansion
1. Project staffs salary
+ Description: static cost for paying salary for implementing team to work for the project, including PM, HR team, marketing team, etc.
+ Estimation: roughly 1.98 billion VND for all staffs within 12 months (PM: 30mVND, designer: 20mVND x 3, HR: 15mVND x 2 and marketing: 15mVND x 3)
+ How to estimate: parametric estimating, Mulltiply the salary of each staff roles with 12 and sum all of it.
2. Location Renting
+ Description: cost for renting the physical location in the city central of each province for better visibility and customer attraction.
+ Estimation: 1.8 billion VND for 5 stores (around 30 million VND for each store to rent critical strategy location)
+ How to estimate: analogous estimating. Based on the exsiting knowledge for renting price in the previous launch new store for another brand project.
3. Constructing materials:
+ Description: cost for buying raw materials (as woods, painting liquid, lights, etc.) based on the interior design template
+ Estimation: around 500 million for each store
+ How to estimate: analgous estimating. Relie on the knowledge advantage of the interior designing team, they provide an estimation base on their working experience.
4. Advertisement:
+ Description: paying for the boosting of promotion campaigns like ads on facebook, tiktoks or recruiting local influencers for advertising brand new store in the each city.
+ Estimation: 500 million VND for entire project.
+ How to estimate: research for the current price for each promotional services on internet and contact to local influencers for implementing these campaigns. Sum all of it together.
5. Contingency budget
+ Description: buffering budget for immergency or unexpected issues.
+ Estimation: 500 million VND
+ How to estimate: calculate the entire expenses and remember to pad some budget for the uncertain problems can happen during the project executing.
