Request 1
1. Project name: Coi Cafe's expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh)

2. Purpose: The brand aims to expand in 5 provinces by opening 1 flagship store in each province.

3. Scope constraints:
- Surveying and selecting premises
- Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
- Recruiting and training local personnel
- Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
- Launching campaigns for each store

Time constraints: 12 months
 
Cost constraints: VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 bllion/store)

Quality constraints: 
- Enough participants for the survey
- A good premises
- Interiors and stores aesthetic consistent with the brand.
- Local personnel with adaquate skills and experiences.
- Ensuring a raw material supply chain
- Insight-driven and effective campaign for each store 
- Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Request 3: 
Risk 1: High renting venue expense
- Description: need of good location for business especially coffee store in central provinces like those cost a quite high budget.
- Possibility: high
- Impact: the over budget for each store may occur and affect the cost constraints.
- Mitigation: scouting the location carefully, back up 2 more location same or lower renting fees, create partnership with other local business.
- Contingency: dealing with the owner of location by creating one and only VIP/ special discount, treatment for them from the brand, parallel to searching the new location from surrounding areas, reduce budget of other activities and adding to location budget.

Risk 2: Missing the store opening schedule

- Description: Time for interiors of each store may longer due to adjusting details and finding materials in some provinces. Another problem to deal with is qualified human resources for each store.
- Impact: behind schedule 
- Mitigation: Select one certain interiors supplier for all stores, creating three-round reviewing for each interior design. Build a consistent, clarified and effeciency recruitment process apply to all stores. 
- Contingency: Meeting with interior design team to find the replacement option. Selecting one employee from internal company to working at that place in one month directly manage and handling problem of that flagship store and must find adaquate personnel for that store at the same timeframe.

Risk 3: Low revenue for the first 3-months 
- Description: Revenue for the first 3-months not meet the expectation, may lead to uncomplete the innitial break-even point in 8 months.
- Impact: Quality constraints
- Mitigation: Find out the local consumption habits and their pain points, building effective marketing plan including online and offline activation. 
- Contigency: Meet with the marketing team to draw an accurate buyer personas and target that group hardly, create buy one get one sale, combo, discount voucher and samples to increase word-of-mouths.
