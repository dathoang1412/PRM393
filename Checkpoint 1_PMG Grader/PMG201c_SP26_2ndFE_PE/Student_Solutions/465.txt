Request 1:

A.Project Name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project

B. Justification

Purpose: to open 1  flagship stores in each of  5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh)

Reason: 
+This coffee brand want to make more money from open more stores not only in the 2 most develop city in Viet Nam but also in the Central of Viet Nam
+This brand want coffee drinker using the reusable cup which is environtment- friendly instead of plastic cups which is more popular and non-environment-friendly
+This brand help Dak Lak farmers by buying their coffee, so if there is more store opening, they can help those farmers more

C.Project constraints : It is to open at least 1 flagship store in each of 5 Cental provinces ( Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) within 12 months. The total budget is VND 8.5 billion which mean VND 1.7  billion each store and each store must reach the break- even point within 8 months after opening

Request 2: Main Cost/budget items:
+Facilities like cafe machine, cashier - machine,...
The store will need facilities to make coffee and other activities that a coffee store need
+ Training employees fees 
The Store will need to pay some money to train the employee for the best service quality for customers
+Place to sell cafe
The Store need to place at a good place to attract more customers 
+Software to manage the store easier
The store need a software like POS or employee manager to decrease the work for the manager and increase the service quality
+Marketing fees on social medias like youtube or facebook
The store need to pay money for advertising itseft to attract more younger who usually use those platforms

Request 3: Project Risk:

Risk 1: Lack of the budget for stores
- The budget VND 8.5  billion might not be enough to handle the target goal for each store
-Possible impacts : 
 +Scope : at least one store will be affected
 +Time: might impact the planned time as we might need to   search for the sponsor or make a meeting to decide again about the budget before continue the project 
 +Cost/Budget : the budget might be bigger or smaller to afford the  project
 +Quality: the quality might have to lower to fit with the    budget

Risk 2: Cannot complete the flagship store goal in 12 month
- For some reasons like training employees take too long, the lack of  budget for develop the store,... make the goal of each province have 1 flagship store can not be done and will take more time to complete
-Possible impacts:
 +Scope: at least one store will be affected
+Time: have to expand the time to complete the planned goal
+ Cost/Budget : Might have some risks of have to paid more money to handle with the risk
+Quality: the quality of each store might be not the same, for example Coi Cafe at Da Nang have a better rating than the one at Binh Dinh

Risk 3: Cannot building a raw material supply chain at some place
- For some reason about the environment , earth,... there are some or all raw materials that the store need do not have a supplier at that area
-Possible impacts:
+Scope:at least one store will be affected
+Time: Take more time to delivery material from another area to the local store
+Cost/Budget: have to pay an extra delivery fee for the materials
+Quality: the lack of material will sometimes happen lead to a low quality service for customers
