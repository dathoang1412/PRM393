- Expansion Of Coi Cafe: Construct and Expand Coi Cafe's Brand Campaign

2 Justifications :
- in context declining of appearance of VietNamese Brand on the market due to apearance of other brands from other countrys such as Starbucks, MacDonald,... , this project aims to promote a sustainable Brand for VIetNamese and special is Coffee


3 Project Contrains
Scope:
- Expand project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province.
- The project includes surveying and selecting premise, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
- Building  a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store.

Time:
The project duration is strictly limited to 12 months, including planing, execution, monitoring, and closing phase, with all campaign activities required to be completed within this fixed timeline

Cost/budget;
The total budget is capped at VND 8.5 billion(approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)  from equity and commercial bank loans covering surveying, designing and constructing the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material and launching campaign.

Quality
Each Store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening and a measurable increase in awareness about Coi Cafe's Brand
