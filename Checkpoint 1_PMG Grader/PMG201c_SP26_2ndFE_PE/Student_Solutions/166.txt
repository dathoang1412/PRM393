1.1. Project name is a Coi Cafe's expansion. Coi Cafe will be expanded into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months.

1.2. -The purpose to implement this project is to expand its nostalgic space, traditional drink to the community, so that everyone can try Coi Cafe from everywhere not just in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City.
-The reason to implement this project is to make Coi Cafe a big brand in coffee bran field. So that it can attract more customers.

1.3. Scope constraints:
Surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with te brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, launching campaings for each store. Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Time constraints:
Expand into 5 Central provinces with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months

Budget:
The total budget is 8.5 billion VND (approximately 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.

Quality:
The project is led by the Market Development Director as PM, coordinating with the interior design team, HR team, and marketing team.
