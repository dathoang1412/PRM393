- Justifications: Coi's management decided  to implement an expansion project in to 5 Central provices, with 1 flagship store each provices

- Project constraints: The project is led by Market Development Director( Project Manager), coordinating with the interior design team, HR team and marketing team

- Time: 12 months
- Budget: Total budget is 8.5 billion VND( 1.7 billionVND/ store) and the budget come from equity and commercial bank loans
- Quality: Each store mus reach the breack-even point within 8 months after opening. Making the store is the place is nostalgic space, traditional drink and sustainable business model
