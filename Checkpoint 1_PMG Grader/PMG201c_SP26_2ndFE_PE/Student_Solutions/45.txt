Request 1:

1.Project name: Coi Cafe Campaign

2.Justifications:
- Problem: Coi cafe is a pure Vietnamese coffee with only 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh. It's still to small with a famous coffee brand like Coi cafe.
- Need: Coi cafe need to expand their brand to many othes province in Viet Nam not only in Ha Noi and Ho Chi Minh.
- Solution: Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces such as: Da nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai and Binh Dinh. Each  province will have a flagship store. 

3.Constraints:
- Scope: The project must includes surveying and selecting premises, designing, constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store.
- Time: within 12 months
- Cost: VND 8.5 billion
 (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store)
- Quality: Each store must reach the break- even point within 8 months after opening.

Request 3:
 
Three project risks are:

Risk 1: Low user/customer  interaction in the campaign
- Impact: Delayed growth of the campaign and brand Coi Cafe
- Mitigation: Improve the designing and constructing
interiors consistent with the brand. This will help the campaign is more interest to customer.
- Contingency: Prepare more than one team design for many choices.

Risk 2: Delayed campaign time line
- Impact: Delayed time line of the campaign
- Mitigation: Prepare campaign schedule more carefully
- Contingency: Reduce the campaign scope to make sure each strore must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening

Risk 3: Behavior of employee in the store is bad
- Impact: Customer will have a bad thinking about brand Coi Cafe after one times come to the store
- Mitigation: Prepare training employee carefully
- Contingency: Collect feedback from customer and re training the employee.
