Request 1:

Name: Expand Coi Cafe's influence into 5 central provinces.

Purpose: To expand Coi Cafe's influence into 5 central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh) by building a store in each location and building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers. This project's purpose is to capture the local coffee market of the 5 aformentioned provinces by adapting to local tastes and using advertisements.

Scope:
1. Select a suitable premise in each of the 5 central provinces.
2. Design and construct store interiors consistent with the Coi Cafe brand.
3. Recruit and train local personnel.
4. Build a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers.
5. Research local preferences in coffee.
6. Adapt to local preferences in cofee.
7. Design and launch openning campaigns for each store.

Constraints:
1. There must be 5 functional stores, 1 in each of the 5 central provinces, within 12 months.
2. Budget is 8.5 billion VND, around 1.7 billion VND for each store.
3. Each store must reach the break-even point, around 1.7 biilion VND, within 8 months after openning.

Assumptions:
1. Local coffe stores cannot significantly compete with Coi Cafe.
2. The 8.5 billion VND budget is enough.
3. There're suitable premises in each central provinces.
4. Material cost, both store construction cost and coffee ingredient cost, are not too expensive compared to the costs of current Coi Cafe stores in Hanoi and HCM city.

Request 3:
Risk 1:
-Name: Coi Cafe's coffee fails to attract customers.
-Description: Coi Cafe's coffee does not satisfy local preferences.
-Impacts: Increase in material and time cost to research and brew coffee that satisfy local tastes.
-Mitigation: Thorough research into local tastes in coffe (what people usually order, how coffee are prepared by locals, etc.).
-Contingency: Reduce scope, imitate the coffee of local competitors.

Risk 2:
-Name: Budget overrun.
-Description: Costs exceed budget.
-Impacts: Reduced coffee quality, reduced personnel training level, less effective opening campaigns.
-Mitigation: Compare expenses with budget, track expenses weekly, search for cheap suppliers.
-Contingency: Reduce scope.

Risk 3:
-Name: Opening campaigns fail to attract customers.
-Description: Opening campaigns fail to attract customers.
-Impacts: Low customer numbers, less profit, more time needed to break-even.
-Mitigation: Thorough research into local preferences in coffee.
-Contingency: Issues clarification of campaign messages, rapid adjustment of coffee and campaign messages.
