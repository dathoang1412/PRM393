REQUEST 1 
1. Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project
2. Purpose
To expand Coi Cafe brand to 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Binh Dinh, Quang Ngai) with 1 flagship store in each province, this is a strategic move to increase market share and also build a larger brand in Vietnam, introducing to a larger customer segment based on diverse geography.
3. Constraints
a. Budget: VND 8.5 billion
b. Time: 12 months
c. Scope:
- Surveying and selecting premises
- Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
- Recruiting and training local personnel
- Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
- Launching campaigns for each store
d. Quality
- Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening

REQUEST 2
1. Store Rent
- Description: The rental fee charged per month for the department store  
- Estimated Cost: VND 30 million
- Estimation Method: Analogous Method
- Person in Charge: Project Manager

2. Interiors Design and Displays
- Description: Design the interiors for the department based on brand guideline and acquire the materials for designing product displayment and store atmosphere
- Estimated Cost: VND 300 million
- Estimation Method: Parametric Method (Store equipments, Table, Banners, Coffee Brewing Machine)
- Person in Charge: Interior design team lead
 
3. Staff training and recruit
- Description: Recruiting part-time jobs for local students and training them for a brief period of time 
- Estimated Cost: VND 40 million
- Estimation Method: Parametric Method ( 10 students x VND 4 million)
- Person in Charge: HR team lead

4. Marketing and Promotion
- Description: Run ads on social media about opening day to attract local customers
- Estimated Cost: VND 150 million
- Estimation Method: Analogous Method
- Person in Charge: Marketing team lead

5. Surveying and Focus Groups
- Description: Select a target group for survey, analyse the interviews and survey result to gain insights about the local customer segment
- Estimated Cost: VND 75 million
- Estimation Method: Analogous Method
- Person in Charge: Marketing team lead

REQUEST 3
1. Budget Overrun (cost)
- Description: Cost exceed the initial budget
- Possibility: Medium
- Impact: Medium (May impact on the quality of activities and stores)
- Contingency plan: Cut out non essential items and ask for sponsors
2. Schedule Delay (time)
- Description: Tasks start late and don't finish on required deadlines, which lead to postponing other tasks
- Possibility: Medium
- Impact: Medium (May impact on the scope and quality)
- Mitigation plan: Design clear schedule and effective communication channel and best practices at workplace
3. Poor sales performance (quality)
- Description: Sales after 8 month after opening doesn't reach the break-even point
- Possibility: High
- Impact: High (May heavily impact on quality)
- Mitigation Plan: Run successful marketing campaign on insights gathered from surveys and in-depth interviews
