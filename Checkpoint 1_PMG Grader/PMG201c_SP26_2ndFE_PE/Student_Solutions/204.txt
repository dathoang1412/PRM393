REQUEST 3 : 
Risk 1 : Unsupported weather's condition
Description: Flood and nonstop rain can affect a lot on the supply chain

Impacts : Flood can stop the delivery which lead to lack of supplyment
 
Mitigation: Fully prepared supplyment for the at least the next month, work with many local suppliers for variety source.

Contingency: Use the supplyment from the storage,contact with any available supplier for the soonest backup

Risk 2 : Employee with bad behavior
Description : A very well-known risk in F&B market

Impacts : Negative affect to customer, one store can bring the whole brand's reputation down, cost money to media control, time to rebuild the brand's name.

Mitigation: Well trained and clear recruitment process, treat employee as partner, hire a supervisor the first few months

Contigency: Media control as soon as possible, talk to customer and apologize , warn the employee but do not make punishment in the first time.

Risk 3 : Bad rating of product's quality on the social media

Description : Quality of the product is the spirit of a F&B market, the fall down of it can put bussiness in a very bad shape

Impacts : Cost a lot of money to recover from the bad reputation, lost customers and potentially suppliers

Mitigation : Use the best ingredients, always check for the fressness before making the product, well trained and test the trainee carefully, adjust to match the local's preference.

Contigency : Flash sale for customer to comeback, run more campaigns immediately adjust the product, bring new breeze to the menu
Hope for the best, plan for the worst
