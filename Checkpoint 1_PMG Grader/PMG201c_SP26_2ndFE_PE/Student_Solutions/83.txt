2. justifications
- Purpose:
+ Implement expansion project into 5 central pronvices ( Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).
+ Building a raw material supply chain from Central Region suppliers.
+ Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand.
+ Launching campaigns for each store

3. Constraints:
- Scope:
+ 5 flagship stores for 5 central provinces including Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh
+ building a raw supply chain from Central region suppliers.
- Time: 12 months
- Budget: 8.5 billion VND ( approximately 1.7 billion VND/ store) .
- Quality: Each store reach break - even point within 8 months after opening,

Request 3: 
three project risks
Risk 1:
- Title: over budget
- description: choosing good places to open a flagship store can led to over budget.
- Impact: high
- Mitigation: prepare careful the budget proposal for each task,connect to local people to get more information in each province, decrease the money that spend on the design Team.
- Contingency: using reserve money, using cheaper furnitures for the coffee stores. 

Risk 2: 
- Title: The amount of customers is low
- Description: because there are a lot of coffee stores around each provinces already. Also, some people may have their own favourite coffee store already.
- Impact: high
- Mitigation: prepare carefully marketing strategy, surveying the local people for more information about their habits in drinking coffee.
- Contingency: make a sale on the opening day. Lower price for people when they go in group, colab with some transportation companies to attract more tourists. 

Risk 3: 
- Title: Human resources
- Description: can't find enough workers, waiters, manger for each store
- Impact: medium
- Mitigation: HR team have to prepare early in the recruiting process. 
- Contingency: increase slightly salary for each position. Focus more on training process for the quality human resources.
