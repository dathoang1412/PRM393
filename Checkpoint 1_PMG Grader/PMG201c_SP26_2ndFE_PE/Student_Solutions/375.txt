Request 1: Project Charter

1. Project name: Huong Coi's 

2. Justification ( purpose & reasons):
This project is a pure Vietnamese coffee brand with 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh, famous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu, and sustainable business model ( using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers ). Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces ( Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh ), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months. The project includes: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel,  building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store. 

3. Project constraints:
- Scope: Communication campaign and activation activities only.
- Time: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.
- Cost: The total budget  is VND 8.5 billion ( approximately VND 1.7 billion/store ) from equity and commercial banks loans. 
- Quality: At least 10 hight - quality media content.
- Reach >= 50,000 user
- Engagement >= 5,000 interactions.
