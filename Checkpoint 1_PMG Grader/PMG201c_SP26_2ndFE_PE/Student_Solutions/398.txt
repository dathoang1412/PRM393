Request 1:
1.Project name: Coi Cafe business expansion.

2. Justifications:
-Purpose: Open up 5 flagship stores in 5 different Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).
-Reasons: Expanding there business into 5 Central Provinces.

3.Project constraints:
-Scope: Get one flagship store in each province within 12 months.
-Time: Each store must reach the break-event point within 8 months after opening.
-Cost/budget: Surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers and launching campaigns for each store. The total budget is VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.
-Quality: Nostalgic space, traditional drink menu and sustainable business model (using reusable cups, purchasing coffee directly from Dak Lak farmers).
