Request 1: 
1.1 Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project in 5 Central Provinces
1.2 Justification: 
      - Purpose: Building and expanding the Coi Cafe         Brand, increase earning cost, and creating                 career oportunities for sales staff, coffee                 vendors, gardeners,... in Vietnam. 
      - Reasons: 
        + Increasing of coffee consuming in Vietnam 
        + The trending of young people working         demand at coffee shops
        + Some central provinces (like Quang Nam,          Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh,..) have less coffee          shop chains that impressive, have good quality          and suitable for mordern consumers.    
1.3 
     1.3.1 Project constraint: 
      -  Expansion in 5 provinces: Da Nang, Hue,        Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh
     - Build 1 flagship store in each province
     - Spending maximum approxiamte VND 1.7 billion        for each store
     1.3.2 In terms of scope: 
     - Surveying and selecting premises
     - Designing and constructing interiors consistent 
       with the brand
     - Recruiting and training local personel 
     - Building raw material supply chain from Central 
       region suppliers
     - Launching campaigns for each store
     1.3.3 Time: 12 months
     1.3.4 Budget: the total budget is VND 8.5 billion          (~ 1.7 billion/store)
     1.3.5 Quality: have a raw material supply       chain from Central region suppliers, each store       must reach the break-even point within 8 months       after opening
