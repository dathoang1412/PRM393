Request 1;
1. Project name: Coi Cafe
2. Justifications: Coi Cafe provides a nostalgic space , traditional drink menu to help the customers have a field trip to the old days while enjoying coffee made directly by Dak Lak farmers. The project also provides an sustainable business model for anyone who wants to invest in this project.
3.
- Scope:1 flagship store in 5 Central provinces(Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).
-Time: 12 months to establish 1 flagship store. 
-Cost/Budget: 8.5 billion VND.
- Quality: Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

+ Recuiting and training local personal.
+ Building a raw material supply chain.
+ Launching campaigns.

Reuqest 3:
- 3 project risks;
+ Unsustainable product quality.
+ Past the 8 months break-even point.
+ Employee management.

1.
- Name: Unsustainable product quality.
- Description: The goods being provided to the customers might not be evenly in terms of quality and the customer's liking which will decrease the amount of revenues the store and project gets. 
- Possible impacts: Will greatly impact the quality, time and cost of the project if not handle carefully.
- Response: Regurlarly checks for quality and asks the opinion of the product on any platform.

2. 
-Name: Past the 8 months break-even point.
- Description: This means the money stream doesn't meet up with the cost of the store which will greatly affect the original budget.
- Possible impacts: Slow down the scope of the project and will need time, money and cost to be higher than expected.
- Response: Continue the investment if past 2 to 3 months and the store doesn't bring much progress then close.

3.
-Name: Employee management.
- Description: The project aims to have as many store as possible in the 5 Central provinces so it might run into some trouble when dealing with the salaries, lack of employees or not meeting up with the standard if not monitored carefully.
- Possible impacts: This will mainly slow down the stream of money and time to form an effective working environment.
- Response: Strictly when hiring a manager for each store and may need extra training time for new employment.
