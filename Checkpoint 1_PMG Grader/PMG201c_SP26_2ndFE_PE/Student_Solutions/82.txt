2.  Justification: Expansion brand into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months

3. Constraints: 
- In term of scope: Surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, launching campaigns for each store.
- Time: 12 months
- Budget: VND 8.5 billions (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) 
- Quality: Each store reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening

REQUEST 3: 

Risk 1: Over budget
- Description: The campaign need to cost more than total budget at begin
- Possible impact: Medium (Cost)
- Mitigation: List down all fees need to pay from before run the project, keeptrack closely for each store
- Contigency: Run the store and use entire revenue to continue operate the project. Also minimize employees in each store to minimize the paid

Risk 2: Store can not reach the break-even point on time
- Description: After 8 months opening, some store can not reach the break-even point because of consumer quantity and other reasons
- Possible impact: High (Quality)
- Mitigation: Push hard the revenue on first week from lauching day, run the marketing ads before opening day to reach as much as possible consumer
- Contigency: Continue to run the store, use revenue from stores which is ahead renevue to run other store

Risk 3: Flagship store in some province can not opening on time due to the reason of store rental. 
- Possible impact: Medium (Time)
- Mitigation: Scouting the rent market at those province at least 6 months before decide to run the project
- Contigency: Do all the prepare for operation while finding rental space. When get a possible space, all other preparation has done and ready to opening
