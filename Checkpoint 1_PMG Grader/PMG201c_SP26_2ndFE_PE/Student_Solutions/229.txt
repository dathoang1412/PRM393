Request 1:
1. Project name: Coi Cafe Expansion Project

2. Justifications (purpose & reasons to implement this project): 

- Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central Provinces
- Surveying and selecting premises
- Designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand
- Recruiting and training local personnel
- Building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
- Launching campaigns for each of the 5 store throughout the 5 Provinces

3. Project constraints, in terms of scope, time, cost/budget, and quality:

- The project must be completed within 12 months.
- The total budget is VND 8.5 billion from equity and commercial bank loans.
- Each store must reach the break-even point within 8 months after opening.

Request 2:
Main cost/budget items and details:

1. Interior designing
- Description: Budget used to pay for interior designers to try and implement new design concepts/styles.
- Estimation: Fixed budget for each applicable, appropriate design for a store.
- Way to estimate: The entire design team will work on each store's interior at a time. After one design has been approved and applied, budget for one design will be paid.
- Person in charged: Leader of the design team

2. Store building
- Description: Construction cost of the 5 stores.
- Estimation: Approximately 50% of budget.
- Way to estimate: The 5 stores would resemble each other. Therefore, fixed budget for each.
- Person in charged: Project Manager be in charged of monitoring construction teams.

3. Training local personnels
- Description: Budget used to pay for trainers.
- Estimation: One trainer will be in charged of one class of local personnels with scheduled training time. Fixed budget for the whole training period.  
- Way to estimate: budget is divided into the number of training sessions, which is fixed at the beginning. 
- Person in charged: Leader of HR team.

4. Building raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers
- Description: Connects and sign contracts with suppliers from the Central region.
- Estimation: Depends on the contract's policies but not over 30% of the budget.
- Person in charged: Project Manager

5. Launching campaigns for each store
- Description: Marketing team will start a campaign attracting customers in these regions via social media platforms such as Facebook/Tiktok/Instagram. Applying opening sales and discounts is also a part of the campaign.
- Estimation: 7% of budget will be spent on 2 on-airs.
- Way to estimate: The two on-airs must attract at least 1500 people interacting on social media platforms. 

Request 3: 
Project risks and details:

1. Trainer drops out unexpectedly
- Description: The trainer, who is responsible for training local personnels, be dropped out unexpectedly due to accident, family issue,...
- Impacts: Quality (Medium), Time (High), Cost (High)
- Response plan: 
 + Mitigation: Have training program well-documented so that the next trainer taking over shortens the time to be qualified for training.
 + Contingency: Have reserved trainers who are ready to join in anytime.

2. Suppliers get bankrupt
- Description: A supplier suddenly gets bankrupt after signing official contract.
- Impacts: Quality (Medium), Time (High), Cost (Medium)
- Response plan: 
 + Contingency: Connects in advance with several different potential suppliers.

3. Copyright issue with interior design
- Description: The interior design of a store coincidentally assembles another firm's logo or design that is formerly registered.
- Impacts: Quality (Medium), Time (Medium), Cost (High)
- Response plan: 
 + Contingency: Do careful research of other firm's logo, symbols or design that might interfere with the project.
