Request 1:
Project name: Coi Cafe expansion

Justification:
Purpose: Implementing 1 flagship store of Coi Cafe brand into 5 provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh).
Reason: market expansion to reach more customers from Central provinces, make brand name more popular and get more revenue.

Constraints:
Scope:
- survey and premises selection.
- Recruiting and training local staff.
- Finding and cooporate with Central region vendors.
- lanch campaigns for each store.
- interiors consistent design and construct.
Time: The project is scheduled to completed within 12 months. It includes all phases of Market expansion, from survey and select premises to launch campaigns for each store.
Cost/Budget: This project's budget is VND 8.5 billion, which means approximately VND 1.7 billion/store. It comes from  equity and commercial bank loans, spending to all activities process of new store in Central region.
Quality: after 8 months of opening, each new store must reach the break-even point and make profit afterthat.
