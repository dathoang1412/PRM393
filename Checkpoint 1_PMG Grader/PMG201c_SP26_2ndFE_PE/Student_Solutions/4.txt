Request 1: Narrative charter
1. Project name: Coi Cafe - Vietnamese coffee brand
2. Justifications:
    Nowadays, coffee plays an important role for people in real life. So that the requirement of people for using coffee increase very high. However, people do not know how to believe into some stores due to the bad quality of coffee.
    This project is created to:
     - Help people have many choices for buying and drinking real coffee.
     - Advertise images of Dak Lak's coffee to everyone to know, and encourage people to use resuable cup to protect environment.
     - Open more stores in other provinces of North and Middle of Vietnam.
3. Project constraints
   In terms of scope:
      - Survey and select premises
      - Design and construct interiors consistent with the brand
      - Recruit and train local people
      - Build a raw material supply from Central region 
      - Launch campaigns for each store.
   => Too many activities, need to work quickly
    
    Time
       - Duration: 12 months with 1 flagship store in each province and 8 months after opening for each store
    => Time is very short, must do it quickly

     Cost/budget
        - Duration: 8.5 billion VND(approximately 1.7 billion on one store)
    => Limited cost for each store, must spend carefully for activites
 
      Quality:
         - Ensure the progress is completed about 70%
         -  Quality of products must be checked before input to coffee store
