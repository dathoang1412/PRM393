REQUEST 1:
1. Project name: Coi Cafe - a pure Vietnamese coffee
2. Justifications:
- Purpose: Coi's management decided to implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces ( Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months
- Reason: Coi Cafe has had 12 stores in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City for a long time. Because Coi is famous for its nostalgic space, traditional drink menu and sustainable business model, Coi's management wants to expand their market, improve their brand and have a lot of news customer in many different provinces.
3. Project constraints:
- Scope: The project includes: surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel, building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and lauching campaigns for each store
- Time: 12 months
Project milestones:
+ Project initiation: Month 1.
+ Project planning : Month 2
+ Project executing ( pre-launch, launch, post-launch) : Month 3- 11
+ Project monitoring: Month 3-11
+ Project closing: Month 12
- Cost: VND 8.5 billion ( approximately VND 1.7 billion/store) from equity and commercial bank loans.
- Quality: Each store must reach the break - even point within 8 months after opening.
