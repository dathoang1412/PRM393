Request 1:
 1. Project Name: Coi Cafe Project
 2. Justifications: This project is implemented to an expansion project into 5 Central provinces (Da Nang, Hue, Quang Nam, Quang Ngai, Binh Dinh), with 1 flagship store in each province within 12 months and each store must reach the break-even point within 8 moonths after opening.
 3. Project Constraints: 
 - Scope: The project must complete main tasks and achieve the goal of implement an expansion project into 5 Central provinces with 1 flagship store in each province.
 - Time: Must be completed within 12 months.
 - Cost: Total budget is strictly limited to VND 8.5 billion (approximately VND 1.7 billion/store).
 - Quality: include surveying and selecting premises, designing and constructing interiors consistent with the brand, recruiting and training local personnel. building a raw material supply chain from Central region suppliers, and launching campaigns for each store.
